Gold & Tips

Pairing

  • Mining (smelt everything to save).
  • Cooking/First Aid for self-sustain while leveling.

Profit

  • Mithril Spurs, Weapon Chains, Rods.
  • Titansteel cooldown crafts (helms/boots/epics).
  • Savage Saronite PvP pieces sell early WotLK.

Cooldowns

  • Titansteel Bar (20h). Use smelt macro in a capital near mailbox.
  • Check daily AH for cheap ores; prospectors raise ore prices.
  • Use mining route in Sholazar/Icecrown for Saronite/Titanium.

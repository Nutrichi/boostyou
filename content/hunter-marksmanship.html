Marksmanship Hunter Leveling Guide

Ranged burst, strong weapon scaling, and solid dungeon damage. MM brings Aimed/Multi-Shot pressure and Trueshot Aura support. Covers Classic, TBC, and WotLK from 1-80.

Updated 2026 Ranged Burst DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why MM in Classic?

Highest ranged burst with Aimed/Multishot, excellent dungeon performance, and strong weapon scaling. Requires more mana management than BM.

Strengths

  • Strong single-target burst; good for tagging/elite burns.
  • Trueshot Aura boosts party melee/ranged.
  • Good dungeon damage if pet holds off-tank or Assist.

Weaknesses

  • More downtime (mana) than BM; less pet tanking.
  • Heavily weapon dependent (ranged DPS).
  • Lower solo safety if pet loses threat.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Lethal Shots 5/5; Aimed Shot (if desired) for openers.
  • Improved Hunter’s Mark; Efficiency 3-5/5.
  • Hawk Eye 3/3 for range if PvP/comfort.

Mid (30-44)

  • Trueshot Aura at 40 (party DPS boost).
  • Mortal Shots 5/5; Barrage 3/3.
  • Go for the Throat (pet focus generation) if pet-focused.

Late (45-60)

  • Improved Aspect of the Hawk (if not in BM) or Ranged Weapon Specialization.
  • Filler: Scatter Shot for control, Deterrence for safety.
  • Beast splash: Unleashed Fury/Frenzy for pet damage.
Playstyle: Pet on target → Mark → Aimed/Auto/Multishot weave; conserve mana with Aspect of the Viper when low.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • Hunter’s Mark → Aimed Shot opener (optional) → Serpent Sting → Arcane/Multi weave.
  • Keep Auto Shots unclipped; use Kill Command.
  • Aspect of the Hawk; swap to Viper when <30% mana.

AoE/Dungeons

  • Multi-Shot on CD; Volley for packs (watch threat).
  • Trap/Freezing for CC; Feign Death to drop threat.
  • Misdirection (not in Classic) skip; rely on pet growl/positioning.

Survival

  • Concussive Shot kiting; Wing Clip + Raptor Strike if needed.
  • Bandage instead of eating; keep pet happy for DPS.
  • Freezing Trap for adds; Deterrence for emergencies.

Why MM in TBC?

MM gains Silencing Shot and Improved Hunter’s Mark for raid support. Leveling is smooth with strong weapon scaling; BM is faster overall, but MM is viable and good practice.

Spec: Deep Marks to Silencing Shot/Trueshot Aura, Mortal Shots, Barrage. Off-tree BM for Unleashed Fury/Frenzy or Survival for Resourcefulness.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-64)

  • Marks: Lethal Shots, Barrage, Aimed Shot (optional), Mortal Shots.
  • Imp Hunter’s Mark; Go for the Throat.

Mid (65-67)

  • Trueshot Aura; Scatter Shot (utility).
  • Silencing Shot at 70 (end of tree); prep points.

Late (68-70)

  • Finalize Silencing Shot; fill Efficiency/Improved Arcane Shot.
  • BM splash for Ferocity/Unleashed Fury or Survival for Clever Traps.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • Hunter’s Mark → Steady/Auto weave; Arcane Shot filler (don’t clip).
  • Aimed Shot opener on elites; Silencing Shot for interrupt/extra damage.
  • Kill Command on proc; Viper when low.

AoE/Dungeons

  • Multi-Shot; Volley when safe; trap for CC.
  • Feign Death to drop threat; Misdirection to tank (TBC).
  • Freezing Trap for caster control; Silencing Shot on casters.

Survival/Pet

  • Keep pet growl off in dungeons; on in solo.
  • Mend Pet on heavy damage; keep happiness up.
  • Wing Clip/Concussive for kiting; Deterrence (if talented).

Why MM in WotLK?

Top-tier burst with Chimera Shot, strong scaling with armor pen/crit, and solid AoE with Piercing Shots. Faster pace but more mana hungry than BM.

Spec: Marks deep to Chimera Shot, Piercing Shots, Improved Steady Shot, Careful Aim. Off-tree BM: Go for the Throat; Survival: Resourcefulness if desired.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-74)

  • Lethal Shots, Mortal Shots, Barrage, Careful Aim.
  • Aimed Shot, Rapid Recuperation (mana sustain).
  • Go for the Throat (pet focus).

Mid (75-77)

  • Chimera Shot; Improved Steady Shot.
  • Readiness for double cooldowns.
  • Trueshot Aura baseline (WotLK).

Late (78-80)

  • Piercing Shots (bleed on crits), Marked for Death.
  • Rapid Killing/Quick Shots for leveling speed.
  • Filler into Survival/BM for utility.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Hunter’s Mark → Serpent Sting → Chimera Shot to refresh.
  • Aimed Shot; Steady/Auto weave; Kill Command on proc.
  • Rapid Fire + Readiness to reset; keep Piercing Shots bleeding.

AoE

  • Multi-Shot; Volley for packs; Explosive Trap if safe.
  • Misdirection to tank/pet; Feign Death after big Volleys.
  • Ice Trap for control; Silencing Shot on casters.

Survival

  • Deterrence for emergencies; Disengage to kite.
  • Aspect of the Viper when low mana; Hawk/Dragonhawk for DPS.
  • Keep pet Mend Pet; use Cower to drop pet threat if needed.

Gear, Weapons, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Agility > Hit (cap) > Crit > Attack Power > Armor Pen (WotLK) > Haste.
  • Ranged weapon DPS is the biggest upgrade.

Weapons

  • Always chase ranged DPS upgrades; melee for stats only.
  • TBC: Nesingwary 4000; Coilfang/Mech drops.
  • WotLK: Quest blues/heroic drops; crafted scopes/guns.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Scope; +Agi to gloves.
  • TBC: Stabilized Eternium Scope; Nethercobra Leg Armor.
  • WotLK: Heartseeker Scope; Icescale Leg Armor; +Agi enchants.

Pro Tips & Mana

Leveling Speed

  • Use Rapid Fire on cooldown; Readiness (WotLK) to double burst.
  • Carry high-DPS ammo; keep quiver/bag upgraded.
  • Stutter-step to avoid clipping autos while moving.

Mana Management

  • Aspect of the Viper when <30% mana; swap back for damage.
  • Use lower rank Arcane Shot if mana starved (Classic/TBC).
  • Drink between long pulls; use Rapid Recuperation (WotLK).

Group Utility

  • Misdirection (TBC/WotLK) to tank; Silencing Shot on casters.
  • Traps for control; pet interrupts if available.
  • Trueshot Aura buff; keep Hunter’s Mark up.

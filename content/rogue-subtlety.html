Subtlety Rogue Leveling Guide

Shadowstep burst, Ambush openers, and high control. Covers Classic, TBC, and WotLK Subtlety paths for 1-80 with stealth-first routing.

Updated 2026 Stealth / Control DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Subtlety in Classic?

Ultimate control and stealth. Ambush/Backstab burst deletes clothies, and you can skip dangerous camps with stealth. Slightly slower than Combat but safest for contested PvP servers.

Strengths

  • Stealth mobility and skip potential; excellent world PvP control.
  • Big opener crits; Find Weakness/Improved Ambush synergy.
  • Improved Sap, Preparation for double cooldowns.

Weaknesses

  • Energy tighter than Combat; sustained DPS lower.
  • Backstab/Ambush positional; dagger reliant.
  • Less AoE/cleave; uptime slower on long grinds.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Improved Sap 3/3 for CC; Opportunity 5/5 for opener damage.
  • Camouflage/Stealth speed; Initiative for extra combo points.
  • Malice 5/5 (Assassination) for crit; Remorseless Attacks for chain leveling.

Mid (30-44)

  • Premeditation (optional) and Preparation for double cooldowns.
  • Ghostly Strike for dodge + damage; Improved Ambush.
  • Serrated Blades for armor reduction; Hemorrhage if you prefer spammable builder.

Late (45-60)

  • Setup/Elusiveness for control and shorter CDs.
  • Fill Assassination with Ruthlessness, Relentless Strikes for energy.
  • Optional: Improved Gouge for control, Improved Sprint for mobility.
Use daggers; Ambush → Backstab chain with Slice and Dice rolling. Hemorrhage is acceptable if you prefer positional freedom.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • Stealth → Sap extra target → Ambush/Backstab opener.
  • Slice and Dice early; Backstab/Hemo builder; Eviscerate/Kidney Shot finisher.
  • Use Premeditation + Cheap Shot for heavy control on elites.

Survival

  • Evasion + Ghostly Strike for dodge stacking.
  • Vanish to reset; Blind for emergency stop; Gouge → Bandage.
  • Crippling Poison for kiting; First Aid maxed.

Stealth/Skips

  • Use Distract to split patrols; Sap priority caster.
  • Abuse Remorseless Attacks crit buff when chain pulling.
  • Pickpocket while leveling for extra gold/lockboxes.

Why Subtlety in TBC?

Shadowstep adds mobility and on-demand burst for PvP/server safety. Still weaker PvE than Combat, but excels at ganking, skips, and controlling difficult quest hubs.

Spec to Shadowstep, Hemorrhage, Serrated Blades, and Preparation. Splash Assassination for Malice, Ruthlessness, and Relentless Strikes to stabilize energy.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-63)

  • Opportunity 5/5, Camouflage, Improved Sap.
  • Malice 5/5; Ruthlessness 3/3; Relentless Strikes.
  • Hemorrhage as main builder if you dislike Backstab positioning.

Mid (64-67)

  • Premeditation + Preparation for double cooldowns.
  • Serrated Blades for armor reduction and rupture value.
  • Shadowstep for gap-close; Honor Among Thieves not yet.

Late (68-70)

  • Improved Kidney Shot for elite control.
  • Enveloping Shadows for mitigation vs AoE/DoTs.
  • Fill with Elusiveness/Dirty Deeds for cheaper openers.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • Stealth → Cheap Shot → Hemorrhage/Backstab spam.
  • Slice and Dice early; Rupture if target lives long; Eviscerate finish.
  • Shadowstep + Ambush for burst on casters; Kick key spells.

Control

  • Sap one, Blind another; Preparation for double CC.
  • Vanish to reset; Cloak of Shadows to clear magic.
  • Shadowstep → Kidney Shot for ranged mobs.

Survival

  • Ghostly Strike + Evasion for dodge stacking.
  • Crippling Poison for kiting; Deadly/Instant main-hand.
  • Bandage often; use food buffs between skirmishes.

Why Subtlety in WotLK?

Honor Among Thieves, Shadow Dance, and Shadowstep make Subtlety explosive. Still behind Combat for PvE cleave, but amazing for PvP, tagging, and high burst questing.

Spec for Hemorrhage, Honor Among Thieves, Shadow Dance, and Filthy Tricks. Off-tree Assassination for Malice, Ruthlessness, Relentless Strikes; optional Improved Poisons for FoK builds.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-74)

  • Opportunity, Improved Ambush, Serrated Blades.
  • Hemorrhage as main builder; Initiative + Premeditation.
  • Assassination splash: Malice, Ruthlessness, Relentless Strikes.

Mid (75-77)

  • Honor Among Thieves for combo point flow.
  • Shadowstep, Filthy Tricks for shorter CDs.
  • Enveloping Shadows/Elusiveness to reduce magic/AoE damage.

Late (78-80)

  • Shadow Dance capstone for back-to-back Ambush chains.
  • Waylay for snares; Deadliness/Finishers to round damage.
  • Optional: Improved Sprint for Icecrown mobility.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Stealth → Premeditation + Cheap Shot → Hemorrhage spam.
  • Slice and Dice early; Rupture (if talented) then Eviscerate.
  • Shadow Dance for Ambush chains; Shadowstep for rears on casters.

AoE/Utility

  • Fan of Knives with double Instant/Deadly for quick tags.
  • Tricks of the Trade to help tanks; Distract/Sap to set up pulls.
  • Shadowstep → Fan for gap-closing AoE tags.

Survival

  • Cloak of Shadows; Evasion; Feint to reduce AoE.
  • Blind + Vanish to reset; Prep for double defensives.
  • Crippling Poison for kiting; keep bandages/pots handy.

Gear, Weapons, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Hit (6-9% leveling) > Agility > Attack Power > Crit > Haste; Expertise helps vs parries.
  • Daggers preferred; fast off-hand for poisons, slow main-hand for Ambush/Backstab.

Weapons

  • Classic: Deadmines/SM/BRD daggers; AV dagger reward.
  • TBC: Blade of the Archmage, Ced's Carver, quest blues.
  • WotLK: Librarian's Paper Cutter (OH), Titansteel Shanker, heroic daggers.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Agility/Crusader; +Hit to gloves if available.
  • TBC: Mongoose or +Agility; +Hit/+AP gloves.
  • WotLK: Berserking/+Agility; Icewalker if hit is low.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • Chain Ambush/Backstab with Slice and Dice up; Hemorrhage when positioning is bad.
  • Use Shadowstep to stick to fleeing mobs; Sprint between pulls.
  • Pickpocket every humanoid for gold/lockboxes; it adds up.

Control

  • Sap/Blind/CS/Kidney rotation lets you solo elites with patience.
  • Gouge → Bandage; Vanish to reset DoTs with Cloak of Shadows.
  • Use Distract on patrols to secure safe quest item pickups.

Group Utility

  • Expose Armor if needed; Tricks of the Trade (WotLK) on tanks.
  • Interrupt chain on casters; peel with Kidney Shot on dangerous mobs.
  • Provide skips with Sap and Distract; Shadowstep to quickly mark targets.

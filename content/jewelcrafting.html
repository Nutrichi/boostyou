Gold & Tips

Pairing

  • Mining for ore to prospect.
  • Alchemy (TBC/WotLK) for transmute epic gems later.

Profit

  • Rare gem cuts after raid unlocks.
  • Icy Prism (RNG blues) early WotLK.
  • Meta gems: Earthsiege/Skyflare for raiders.

Daily Tokens

  • Do JC dailies in Shattrath (1 token/day) and Dalaran (WotLK).
  • Buy high-demand cuts first: Red (Runed/Delicate/Bold) then yellow (Quick/Brilliant).

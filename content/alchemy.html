Gold Tips, Cooldowns, and Pairings

Pairing

  • Herbalism (Lifeblood HoT + self-farmed herbs).
  • Fishing (Pygmy Suckerfish for Pygmy Oil).

Cooldowns

  • Classic: Arcanite (24h).
  • TBC: Primal Might / single primal transmutes (24h).
  • WotLK: Titanium Bar / epic gems (20h); Research (3 days).

Profit Plays

  • Raid-night flasks and Potion of Speed/Wild Magic (WotLK).
  • Resistance pots before new raids (TBC/Classic).
  • Sell discoveries early; prices drop fast.

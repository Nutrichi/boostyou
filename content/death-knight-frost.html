Frost Death Knight Leveling Guide

Control-heavy melee with strong cleave, Howling Blast burst, and great group buffs. Dual wield or 2H options both level quickly.

Updated 2026 Melee Cleave DPS / Tank Offspec Supports WotLK

Why Frost in WotLK?

Excellent control, big AoE with Howling Blast, and great snap threat for tanking. Dual Wield procs add smooth damage.

Strengths

  • Howling Blast + Pestilence for AoE.
  • Great control: Chains of Ice, Mind Freeze.
  • Solid tanking with Frost Presence/Frigid Dreadplate.

Weaknesses

  • More button-heavy than Blood.
  • Depends on weapon quality.
  • Starts at 55 only.

Talents by Level (55-80)

Early (55-63)

  • Blade Barrier; Improved Icy Touch.
  • Annihilation to allow Obliterate without losing diseases.
  • Two-Hand Spec or Nerves of Cold Steel if DW.

Mid (64-73)

  • Howling Blast core AoE.
  • Rime + Killing Machine synergy.
  • Frigid Dreadplate for tankiness.

Late (74-80)

  • Tundra Stalker for damage on diseased targets.
  • Acclimation/Unbreakable Armor optional.
  • Pick up Epidemic from Unholy for longer diseases.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • IT → PS → Obliterate → Blood Strike x2 → Frost Strike dump.
  • Use Rime procs for free HB; KM procs on FS/OB.
  • Keep diseases up; use Empower Rune Weapon for burst.

AoE

  • DnD → HB → Pestilence → Blood Boil.
  • HB on proc/CD; Frost Strike dump RP.
  • Howling Blast + KM melts packs.

Survival

  • Unbreakable Armor for mitigation (if taken).
  • IBF/AMS on cooldowns; Rune Tap if Blood sub-spec.
  • Chains of Ice to kite; Death Strike if low.

Gear, Stats, and Enchants

Stat Priority

  • Hit/Expertise to cap > Strength > Crit > Haste.
  • Defense to 540 if tanking heroics.

Weapons

  • DW: fast/slow combo with Razorice/FC runes.
  • 2H: slow 2H with FC; upgrade often.

Enchants

  • Runeforge: Fallen Crusader main; Razorice off-hand for DW.
  • Strength/AP enchants where possible.
  • Armor kits/defense if tanking.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • HB on cooldown; Pestilence spreads diseases.
  • Use Death Grip to group mobs for HB.
  • Keep Presence set: Frost for tanking, Unholy for speed if DPS.

Group Utility

  • Improved Icy Talons haste buff.
  • Mind Freeze, Strangulate interrupts.
  • Death Grip repositioning.

Survival

  • AMS/IBF often; use Lichborne + DC self-heal if needed.
  • Rune Tap if Blood sub; Death Strike on low HP.
  • Bandages/pots; use Chains of Ice to kite.

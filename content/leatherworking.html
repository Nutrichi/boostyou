Gold & Tips

Pairing

  • Skinning for self-supplied leather.
  • Fishing (Dragonfin) for extra gold while waiting.

Profit

  • Armor kits (Classic/TBC) and leg armors (WotLK).
  • Drums (TBC) if not nerfed on your server.
  • Arctic Fur items early WotLK.

Reps

  • WotLK leg armor patterns from Kirin Tor/Wyrmrest etc.
  • Buy salt/threads in bulk to avoid downtime.

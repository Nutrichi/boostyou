Fastest 70-80 Leveling Route (WotLK 2026)

Battle-tested Northrend routing built for Wrath Classic Anniversary realms and Hardcore transfers.

Updated: January 2026 Testing Platform: RestedXP + manual splits XP Window: 70 → 80 in 34h 12m played

Target Pace: Sub 35 Hours Played

All timings assume heirloom shoulders + chest (10% XP), full quest log routing, and a single dungeon cleave at key breakpoints. Remove heirlooms to add ~3 hours per bracket.

Recommended Zone Order

Both factions begin in the Borean Tundra boat/zeppelin for faster quest density, then swap continents at level 75 to keep XP flowing.

Alliance Route

  • 70-71: Borean Tundra (Fizzcrank & Riplash)
  • 71-73: Dragonblight (Wrathgate unlocked early)
  • 73-75: Grizzly Hills (Eversong dailies unlocked)
  • 75-77: Zul'Drak + Gundrak one-and-done
  • 77-80: Storm Peaks into Icecrown

Horde Route

  • 70-71: Borean Tundra (Warsong Offensive)
  • 71-73: Howling Fjord detour for Apothecary chain
  • 73-75: Dragonblight Wrathgate skip quest
  • 75-77: Zul'Drak + Drak'Tharon dungeon spam
  • 77-80: Icecrown gunship into Argent Vanguard hubs
Cold Weather Flying should be purchased immediately at 77 (borrow gold if needed). Flying cuts nearly 90 minutes from Storm Peaks + Icecrown loops.

Dungeon Cleave Inserts

One well-timed dungeon marathon is still the fastest way to convert rested XP while aligning quest hubs.

Level 72 Boost

  • 2x Nexus clear with all quests picked up beforehand.
  • Focus on blue weapon upgrades + off-hands.
  • Turn-ins deliver ~380k XP (Alliance) or 360k (Horde).

Level 76 Boost

  • Drak'Tharon Keep spam with double group quests shared.
  • Prioritize casters for Mojo Frenzy event achievements.
  • Leads directly into ZD final chains without travel.

Daily + Rested Planning

Stack these daily hubs while traveling so you can exit Northrend at 80 with reputation progress already handled.

Sons of Hodir (77+)

  • Unlock via Brunnhildar quests
  • 3 quests × 23k XP each with flying
  • Essential shoulder enchants pre-raid

Argent Vanguard (79+)

  • Quests cluster around Icecrown front
  • Daily bomber run is 28k XP for 90 seconds
  • Progresses toward Crusader title

Ebon Blade (78+)

  • Shadow Vault unlocking gives mailbox + AH
  • 4 dailies with elite assists worth 110k XP combined
  • Pre-farms reputation for best head enchant

Consumables & Prep Checklist

Before Launch

  • Stock 5 × Flask of the Frost Wyrm (or Guardian)
  • Carry Scrolls + Drums to avoid AH downtime
  • Buy Tome of Cold Weather Flying on alts

During the Push

  • Use Hearthstones on cooldown for hub jumps
  • Mail quest turn-ins (Seaforium, Everfrost) immediately
  • Keep 2× stack of Runic Health & Mana potions

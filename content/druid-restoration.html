Restoration Druid Leveling Guide

HoT-focused healer with unmatched mobility. Dungeon leveling is effortless; solo is slower but very safe with kiting and heals.

Updated 2026 Healer / Support Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Restoration in Classic?

Rejuvenation/Regrowth + swift heals, excellent mobility, and battle rez. Dungeon healers are always wanted.

Strengths

  • HoTs prevent pushback.
  • Innervate/Rebirth utility.
  • Travel Form mobility.

Weaknesses

  • Solo kill speed low.
  • Mana can dip before Innervate.
  • No shield to stop burst damage.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Improved Rejuv; Nature's Focus.
  • Omen of Clarity (if available) for free casts.
  • Furor (Resto/Balance) for shifting.

Mid (30-44)

  • Gift of Nature for healing bonus.
  • Swiftmend for instant heal.
  • Intensity/Reflection for regen.

Late (45-60)

  • Nature's Swiftness for clutch heal.
  • Improved Regrowth; Tranquility use on big pulls.
  • Optional: Improved MotW/Thorns.
Downrank Healing Touch for efficiency; keep Rejuv/Regrowth rolling and drink while moving with Travel Form.

Rotation & Playstyle

Solo

  • Moonfire → Wrath; root + DoT if needed.
  • Heal with Rejuv/Regrowth; Bear for tough mobs.
  • Use Innervate on self early.

Healing

  • Rejuv/Regrowth blankets; HT/Swiftmend for spikes.
  • NS + HT emergency; Tranquility for AoE damage.
  • Keep MotW/Thorns on tank.

Utility

  • Innervate, Battle Rez.
  • Root/hibernate for control.
  • Decurse/Remove poison.

Why Restoration in TBC?

Lifebloom + Swiftmend gives efficient tank healing. Tree of Life aura boosts healing; excellent dungeon healer.

Spec: Tree of Life, Lifebloom, Swiftmend, Nature's Swiftness, Intensity. Improved MotW/Thorns optional.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-63)

  • Lifebloom; Improved Rejuv.
  • Nature's Swiftness.
  • Intensity for regen.

Mid (64-67)

  • Tree of Life form; Improved ToL aura.
  • Empowered Rejuv; Living Spirit.
  • Swiftmend for instant heals.

Late (68-70)

  • Natural Perfection or Subtlety.
  • Gift of Nature maxed.
  • Balance splash: Nature's Grasp/Control if needed.

Rotation (TBC)

Healing

  • Lifebloom stacks on tank; Rejuv/Regrowth upkeep.
  • Swiftmend for spikes; NS + HT emergency.
  • Innervate when low; drink between pulls.

Solo

  • Moonfire/Wrath; Bear for survivability.
  • Keep Lifebloom rolling on self while pulling.
  • Root/hibernate for control.

Utility

  • Battle Rez; MotW/Thorns.
  • Remove Curse/Poison; Cyclone for control.
  • Tree aura boosts party healing taken.

Why Restoration in WotLK?

Wild Growth and Nourish make Resto incredibly flexible. Excellent dungeon/raid healer with top-tier HoTs and mobility.

Spec: Wild Growth, Revitalize, Nourish, Living Seed, Gift of the Earthmother. Balance splash for Nature's Grace optional.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-73)

  • Empowered Rejuv; Living Spirit.
  • Wild Growth unlock; Improved Rejuv.
  • Nature's Splendor for HoT duration.

Mid (74-77)

  • Revitalize for mana/energy to party.
  • Living Seed; Nature's Grace (Balance splash) if desired.
  • Gift of the Earthmother for GCD reduction.

Late (78-80)

  • Nourish for spot healing synergy.
  • Tranquility buffs; Improved Barkskin.
  • Fill with Subtlety/Tranquil Spirit for efficiency.

Rotation (WotLK)

Healing

  • Rejuv/Regrowth on tank; Lifebloom stack if needed.
  • Wild Growth on cooldown; Nourish spot heals.
  • Swiftmend for instant top-off; Innervate on cooldown.

Solo/Questing

  • Moonfire/Wrath; keep HoTs on self.
  • Bear/Cat for tagging; Travel Form speed.
  • Wild Growth for kiting packs; Barkskin while channeling Hurricane if needed.

Utility

  • Battle Rez; MotW/Thorns; Revitalize resource return.
  • Remove Curse/Poison; Cyclone/Root.
  • Tranquility on big pulls; Barkskin pre-cast.

Gear, Stats, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Spirit/Intellect > Spell Power > Haste (WotLK) > Crit.
  • MP5 useful early; Stamina for safety.

Weapons

  • Staff/off-hand with spell power and spirit.
  • Classic: SM/BRD caster staves.
  • TBC: Coilfang/Badge maces/off-hands.
  • WotLK: Titansteel Guardian; badge off-hands.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Healing Power; +Spirit to weapon.
  • TBC: Major Intellect/Healing; +Spirit chest.
  • WotLK: +63 SP/Black Magic; +Spirit/+Int to gear; Icewalker if hit needed for solo.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • Queue dungeons for fastest XP; you always get in.
  • Use Travel Form to reduce downtime; keep HoTs rolling while moving.
  • Innervate early; drink during travel.

Group Utility

  • Battle Rez, Innervate, MotW/Thorns.
  • Revitalize resource returns (WotLK).
  • Root/Cyclone for control; remove poison/curse.

Survival

  • Barkskin pre-Hurricane; bear for emergencies.
  • NS + HT saves tanks; Tranquility for big AoE.
  • Carry pots/bandages; Wild Growth to stabilize.

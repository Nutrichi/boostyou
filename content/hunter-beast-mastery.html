Beast Mastery Hunter Leveling Guide

Pet-tanked safety, sustained DPS, and high uptime. BM is the fastest and safest Hunter leveling spec across Classic, TBC, and WotLK. Talents, pets, rotations, gear, and routes for 1-80.

Updated 2026 Pet-Tanked / Ranged DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why BM in Classic?

Pet tanks for you, minimal downtime, and high sustained damage with Kill Command + Bestial Wrath. Great for solo questing and fast dungeon clears.

Strengths

  • Safest leveling: pet holds threat, you take almost no damage.
  • High uptime; minimal drinking/eating.
  • Simple rotation; great for open-world farming.

Weaknesses

  • Pet management required (happiness, training).
  • Lower burst than MM; reliant on pet tanking well.
  • Weapons matter; aim for ranged upgrades often.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Improved Aspect of the Hawk 5/5; Focused Fire 2/2.
  • Endurance Training 5/5; Unleashed Fury 5/5.
  • Put points toward Ferocity (pet crit) and Thick Hide.

Mid (30-44)

  • Bestial Wrath at 40 (core).
  • Frenzy 5/5 for pet attack speed.
  • Spirit Bond 2/2 for passive healing.

Late (45-60)

  • Ferocity/Improved Revive Pet; Catlike Reflexes for pet avoidance.
  • Marks splash: Lethal Shots 5/5, Aimed Shot optional for pulls.
  • Efficiency 3/5+ for less mana use.
Core loop: Send pet → Hunter’s Mark → Serpent Sting → Auto/Arcane Shot weave → Bestial Wrath on elites/bosses.

Rotation & Pets

Single Target

  • Maintain Hunter’s Mark; Serpent Sting on long targets.
  • Arcane Shot on cooldown; Auto weave (no shot clipping).
  • Use Kill Command whenever available.

Pets

  • Leveling pets: Cats/Boars early; Cats/Raptors for DPS; Turtles/Bears for tankiness.
  • Keep pet Growl maxed; train Bite/Claw accordingly.
  • Carry pet food, keep happiness high for damage bonus.

Survival/Uptime

  • Aspect of the Cheetah for travel; Hawk for combat.
  • Feign Death to drop threat; Bandage between pulls instead of eating.
  • Use traps for control on adds (Freezing/Ice Trap).

Why BM in TBC?

BM is top-tier for TBC leveling and raiding. Bestial Wrath + Serpent’s Swiftness push huge pet damage; Kill Command scales well.

Spec: Deep BM to Serpent’s Swiftness 5/5 and The Beast Within. Marks splash: Lethal Shots, Efficiency; optionally Improved Hunter’s Mark for melee comps.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-64)

  • Finish Frenzy 5/5; Bestial Wrath already.
  • Spirit Bond 2/2; Improved Revive Pet.
  • Animal Handler for pet expertise (threat/damage).

Mid (65-67)

  • Serpent’s Swiftness 5/5 (huge power spike).
  • The Beast Within for CC immunity/extra damage.
  • Marks: Efficiency/Lethal Shots for mana/crit.

Late (68-70)

  • Go for Improved Hunter’s Mark or Go for the Throat (pet focus generation).
  • Catlike Reflexes if your pet tanks a lot.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • Hunter’s Mark → Send pet → Steady Shot/Auto with Arcane Shot weaved (avoid clipping autos).
  • Kill Command on proc; Bestial Wrath on CD for elites/bosses.
  • Maintain Aspect of the Hawk; Viper only when low mana.

AoE/Dungeons

  • Multi-Shot on CD; Volley for packs (watch threat).
  • Misdirection if available (raid/group) to tank.
  • Freezing Trap for CC; Feign Death to drop threat.

Pet & Survival

  • Keep pet Mend Pet up on heavy damage; healers love it.
  • Use Viper Sting on casters for mana returns (minor).
  • Bandage between pulls; Disengage (not in TBC baseline) skip.

Why BM in WotLK?

BM is safer but behind MM/Survival at endgame; still excellent for leveling with fast pet damage and The Beast Within for CC immunity.

Spec: Serpent’s Swiftness, Bestial Wrath/Beast Within, Cobra Strikes, Longevity, Invigoration. Off-tree: Marks for Lethal Shots/Go for the Throat.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-74)

  • Frenzy 5/5; Focused Fire; Improved Revive Pet.
  • Beast Within; Serpent’s Swiftness.
  • Marks: Lethal Shots 5/5.

Mid (75-77)

  • Cobra Strikes (pet crits from your crits).
  • Longevity (cooldown reduction) + Invigoration for mana.
  • Improved Aspect of the Hawk or Focused Aim if hit needed.

Late (78-80)

  • Beast Mastery (exotic pets) optional; not mandatory for leveling.
  • Kindred Spirits for pet damage/movement.
  • Filler: Catlike Reflexes, Animal Handler.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Hunter’s Mark → Serpent Sting → Steady/Auto with Arcane Shot; Kill Command on proc.
  • Bestial Wrath/Beast Within on CD; Readiness (if available) to reset.
  • Keep pet on aggressive target swapping only when safe.

AoE

  • Explosive Trap + Multi-Shot; Volley on packs (watch threat).
  • Misdirection to pet or tank; Feign Death after big Volleys.
  • Ice Trap for kiting; Deterrence if caught.

Pets/Survival

  • Use Tenacity pets for tanking (Turtle/Bear); Ferocity for speed (Cat/Wolf).
  • Mend Pet always ticking on heavy pulls.
  • Aspect of the Viper when low mana; Hawk/Dragonhawk for damage.

Gear, Weapons, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Agility > Hit (3-8% range depending on level) > Crit > AP > Stamina.
  • Ranged weapon DPS is king; upgrade as priority.

Weapons

  • Always prioritize ranged DPS; melee only matters for stats.
  • Classic: Venomstrike, Outrunner’s Bow equivalents, AV gun.
  • TBC: Hellfire/Coilfang bows; Nesingwary 4000.
  • WotLK: Quest blues/heroic drops; crafted Engineering guns.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Scope; +Agility to gloves.
  • TBC: Stabilized Eternium Scope; +10 Agi to gloves; Nethercobra Leg Armor.
  • WotLK: Heartseeker Scope; Icescale Leg Armor; +Agi enchants.

Pro Tips & Pet Management

Leveling Speed

  • Chain pull: send pet early; start next target while pet finishes.
  • Keep ammo/arrows stocked; use highest DPS ammo.
  • Use Aspect of the Cheetah between pulls; cancel on combat.

Pet Care

  • Train pet skills regularly; keep Growl max rank.
  • Maintain happiness (food) for 125% damage.
  • Use Tenacity pets for tough elites; Ferocity for speed runs.

Group Utility

  • Misdirection (TBC/WotLK) to tank/pet.
  • Traps for CC; Aspect of the Wild (Nature res) situational.
  • Pet interrupts (Boar Charge, Gorilla Pummel) on casters.

Gold & Tips

Pairing

  • Herbalism to self-supply herbs for milling.
  • Bank alt near Ink Trader for swaps.

Profit

  • Popular glyphs (major DPS specs) sell fast.
  • Darkmoon Cards (Snowfall Ink) for trinkets.
  • Off-hands: Faces of Doom, Rituals of the New Moon.

Cooldowns

  • Northrend Inscription Research (20h).
  • Minor Inscription Research (low-level glyphs).
  • Snowfall Ink for high-end glyphs/cards—save them.

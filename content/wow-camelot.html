WoW Camelot: The Classic+ Leak, Explained

Everything we know about Blizzard's mysterious "Camelot" project — the leaked timeline, the datamined evidence, and why the community is convinced this is WoW Classic Plus.

Updated: July 7, 2026 Status: Unannounced — leak-based coverage Expected reveal: BlizzCon 2026 (September)

TL;DR — What Is WoW Camelot?

"Camelot" is the code name for an unannounced World of Warcraft branch that appeared on a leaked internal Blizzard timeline and in datamined license data. The community's leading theory: it's WoW Classic Plus — Classic Azeroth with brand-new content instead of another expansion re-run.

Where the Camelot Leak Came From

The name "Camelot" first surfaced through datamining of Blizzard's backend, where fans discovered references to Heroic and Epic edition licenses tied to a product called Camelot — the same edition structure Blizzard uses for standalone launches. Shortly after, a leaked internal product timeline placed "Camelot" in Q3 2026, right around BlizzCon.

What makes this leak different from the usual rumor mill is its track record. The same document correctly predicted, ahead of time:

The Evidence That Camelot Is Classic+

The 1.60 version jump

  • WoW Classic currently runs on version 1.15.x
  • Camelot references point to version 1.60
  • A jump that large signals a heavily modernized Classic engine, not a routine patch

Heroic & Epic licenses

  • Datamined store licenses use standalone-launch structure
  • Suggests Camelot is sold as its own product/event
  • Mirrors how Classic Anniversary launches were packaged

NDA campus visits

  • Content creators recently visited Blizzard HQ under tight NDAs
  • Timing lines up with a BlizzCon 2026 mega-reveal
  • Classic creators reportedly among the invited
Why "Classic Plus"? Classic+ is the community's term for a version of original WoW that gets new content — new quests, dungeons, raids, maybe finished cut content like the Emerald Dream or Karazhan Crypts — instead of simply progressing into TBC/WotLK re-runs. It has been the most requested Classic feature since 2019.

Is WoW Camelot the same as Classic Plus?

That's the leading theory, supported by the 1.15 → 1.60 version jump, datamined Heroic/Epic edition licenses, and its standalone slot on the leaked timeline — but it's not confirmed until Blizzard says so.

When will WoW Camelot be revealed or released?

The leaked timeline says Q3 2026, and BlizzCon 2026 runs in September. Content creator NDA visits to the Blizzard campus strongly suggest a formal BlizzCon reveal.

Fishing Leveling Guide

Relaxed 1-600 fishing route with the right pools, lures, and daily quests to profit while you skill up.

Updated 2026 Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK / Cata / MoP Pairs with Cooking

Classic Route (1-300)

SkillLocationNotes
1-75Starter zonesAny pond. Use Shiny Bauble lure to reduce junk.
75-150Westfall/Darkshore/Loch ModanCatch Oily Blackmouth/Deviate Fish for gold.
150-225Hinterlands/TanarisFirefin/Sagefish pools; buy Strong Fishing Pole.
225Nat Pagle Quest (Dustwallow)Complete artisan quest for 225+ cap.
225-300Feralas/Tanaris/StranglethornStonescale Eel and Nightfin Snapper sell well.
Buy a Strong Fishing Pole or Nat Pagle's Extreme Angler (+25) from the Stranglethorn Fishing Extravaganza. Always use Bright Baubles/Strong Lures near your cap.

TBC Route (300-375)

SkillLocationNotes
300-325ZangarmarshFangtooth Herring/Sporefish; very low junk rate.
325-350Terokkar (lakes)Use +75 lures; do not fish Highland pools yet.
350-375Highland Mixed Schools (Terokkar)Requires flying; yields Primal Water motes + Furious Crawdad.
Do the Old Man Barlo fishing daily near Shattrath for Sharpened Fish Hooks and rare pets. Master Fishing learned at 300 from any Outland fishing trainer.

WotLK Route (375-450)

SkillLocationNotes
375-405Borean Tundra / Howling FjordNettlefish/Musselback Sculpin in coastal pools.
405-430Dragonblight / Grizzly HillsGlacial Salmon and Fangtooth Herring pools.
430-450Sholazar Basin / IcecrownTry Nettlefish pools or Dragonfin Angelfish (for Dragonfin Filets).
Grab the Dalaran fishing daily at 375 for Jewel of the Sewers coins, Weather-Beaten Fishing Hat, and +lure bobber. Grand Master Fishing learned in Howling/Borean/Dalaran.

Cata Fishing (450-525)

SkillZone/SourceNotes
450-500Any Cata zone open waterSkill gain continues anywhere; catch Deepsea Sagefish for Cooking.
500-525Twilight Highlands / Uldum poolsFathom Eel and Highland Guppy pools feed raid food.
The capital fishing dailies (added in Cata) pay skill-ups, gold, and rare rewards. Volatile Water from fishing pools sells to crafters — fish pools whenever you pass one.

MoP Fishing (525-600)

SkillZone/SourceNotes
525-575Any Pandaria water — Golden CarpGolden Carp bites everywhere and always.
575-600Krasarang / Valley pools — Emperor Salmon, Krasarang PaddlefishFeeds the Way of the Steamer and feasts.
Join the Anglers dailies in Krasarang Wilds immediately — rep unlocks the Azure Water Strider (walk-on-water mount) and fishing gear. Find Nat Pagle there for his rare-fish reputation line.

Gear & Tips

  • Use the best lure you have (Aquadynamic Fish Attractor +100) near cap to reduce grey catches.
  • Keep a few Savory Deviate Delight for fun while leveling; they sell well in Classic.
  • Pair with Cooking to convert fish into profitable raid foods (Dragonfin Filet, Fish Feast).
  • Fish during off-hours to avoid empty pools. In WotLK, take Cold Weather Flying for Icecrown pools.

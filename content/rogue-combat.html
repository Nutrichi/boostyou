Combat Rogue Leveling Guide

Blade Flurry cleave, Adrenaline Rush burst, and weapon-flexible leveling. Covers Classic, TBC, and WotLK Combat paths for 1-80.

Updated 2026 Melee Cleave DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Combat in Classic?

Fastest leveling rogue in Classic thanks to weapon flexibility, Blade Flurry cleave, and Adrenaline Rush windows. Excellent sustained damage and uptime with minimal positional demands.

Strengths

  • Blade Flurry + Adrenaline Rush for huge 2-target cleave.
  • Sword/fist/mace options; less reliant on daggers/positioning.
  • Consistent energy flow; strong sustained single-target.

Weaknesses

  • Gear dependent—needs weapon upgrades to feel smooth.
  • Limited AoE beyond Blade Flurry window.
  • Poison management still required; downtime without food/bandages.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Improved Sinister Strike 2/2, Deflection 5/5.
  • Precision 5/5 for hit, Endurance 2/2.
  • Riposte for control; Dual Wield Spec 5/5.

Mid (30-44)

  • Blade Flurry at 30 for cleave windows.
  • Sword Spec or Mace Spec based on weapons.
  • Weapon Expertise; Aggression for Sinister Strike/Eviscerate.

Late (45-60)

  • Adrenaline Rush for massive energy burst.
  • Fill with Vitality, Improved Kick (if needed), and Precision overflow.
  • Optional: Improved Sprint for mobility, Improved Gouge for control.
Use fast off-hand for poisons, slow main-hand for bigger Sinister Strike/Eviscerate. Keep Slice and Dice rolling; Blade Flurry + AR for elite burns or dense packs.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • Stealth → Cheap Shot or Ambush → Sinister Strike spam.
  • Slice and Dice at 2-3 CP, maintain; Eviscerate for finish.
  • Kick heals/casts; Gouge → Bandage to reset.

Survival

  • Evasion for melee packs; Vanish to reset or skip.
  • Sprint to kite; First Aid maxed; health pots for elites.
  • Crippling Poison off-hand for control when needed.

Cleave/AoE

  • Blade Flurry + Adrenaline Rush every 2 min for 2-3 mob burns.
  • Position mobs in front; use evasion if tanking briefly.
  • Use Sweeping Strikes-style planning: pull in pairs to maximize BF.

Why Combat in TBC?

Best all-round rogue spec for leveling and dungeons. Blade Flurry + Adrenaline Rush crushes packs, Combat Potency smooths energy, and weapon options stay flexible.

Spec deep Combat for Combat Potency, Surprise Attacks, and Adrenaline Rush. Splash Assassination for Malice, Ruthlessness, Lethality. Choose Sword/Fist Spec based on drops (Latro’s, Felsteel Longblade).

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-63)

  • Precision 5/5; Dual Wield Spec 5/5.
  • Blade Flurry at 61 if not taken; Aggression for SS/Evis.
  • Weapon Spec (Sword/Fist) based on your best drops.

Mid (64-67)

  • Vitality and Combat Potency (huge energy on OH hits).
  • Adrenaline Rush + BF combo every 2 minutes.
  • Assassination splash: Malice 5/5, Ruthlessness 3/3, Lethality 5/5.

Late (68-70)

  • Surprise Attacks capstone to smooth damage.
  • Fill with Relentless Strikes/Improved Poisons if energy/poisons need help.
  • Improved Kick optional for dungeons/heroics.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • Stealth → Cheap Shot → Sinister Strike spam.
  • Slice and Dice at 2-3 CP; maintain; Eviscerate/Kidney for finish.
  • Use Adrenaline Rush for elites; Kick key casts.

AoE/Cleave

  • Blade Flurry every pull with 2+ mobs; line up with AR for burst.
  • Fan of Knives not yet—focus on cleave, not true AoE.
  • Sap/Blind one target to stabilize if healer is weak.

Survival

  • Cloak of Shadows to clear magic; Evasion for physical bursts.
  • First Aid and food buffs; pop potions on tough pulls.
  • Crippling off-hand for kiting; Deadly/Instant main-hand.

Why Combat in WotLK?

Top-tier cleave with Killing Spree, Blade Flurry, and solid single-target. Combat Potency + Hack and Slash keeps energy flowing, making leveling smooth and fast.

Spec into Hack and Slash/Close Quarters depending on weapons, Combat Potency, Adrenaline Rush, Killing Spree. Off-tree Assassination for Malice/Ruthlessness/Lethality and Improved Poisons if needed.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-74)

  • Precision, Dual Wield Spec, Aggression.
  • Blade Flurry, Adrenaline Rush; Combat Potency.
  • Hack and Slash 5/5 (or CQC) based on swords/fists.

Mid (75-77)

  • Vitality; Unfair Advantage/Throwing Spec optional utility.
  • Surprise Attacks to smooth damage.
  • Assassination splash: Malice, Ruthlessness, Lethality, Relentless Strikes.

Late (78-80)

  • Killing Spree as capstone for burst/AoE tagging.
  • Fill with Improved Kick, Savage Combat, or Weapon Expertise.
  • Optional: Improved Sprint for mobility in ICC zones.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Stealth → Garrote → Sinister Strike spam.
  • Slice and Dice at 2-3 CP; Rupture (if talented) then Eviscerate.
  • Killing Spree on cooldown for burst/tagging; Adrenaline Rush for elites.

AoE/Cleave

  • Fan of Knives at 80 for true AoE; double Instant/Deadly.
  • Blade Flurry + Killing Spree for 3-5 mob melts.
  • Tricks of the Trade on tank/partner when grouping.

Survival

  • Cloak of Shadows for magic; Evasion for melee; Feint to reduce AoE.
  • Sprint/Prep for escapes; Crippling for kiting casters.
  • Vanish to reset dangerous pulls; keep bandages/pots ready.

Gear, Weapons, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Hit (6-9% leveling) > Agility > Attack Power > Crit > Haste; Expertise helps smooth damage.
  • Slow main-hand for Sinister Strike/Killing Spree; fast off-hand for poison procs/Combat Potency.

Weapons

  • Classic: Deadmines/SM/BRD swords; AV rewards.
  • TBC: Felsteel Longblade, Latro's weapons, quest fists/swords.
  • WotLK: Crafted Titansteel Shanker/Steel, Heroic drops, Librarian's Paper Cutter off-hand.

Enchants

  • Classic: Crusader/+Agility; +Hit gloves if available.
  • TBC: Mongoose/+Agility; +Hit or +AP to gloves.
  • WotLK: Berserking/+Agility; Icewalker if hit is low.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • Chain pull with Slice and Dice rolling; pool energy before Blade Flurry + AR.
  • Pick fights with 2 mobs to maximize BF uptime.
  • Use Sprint between pulls; keep Thistle Tea for burst (Classic).

Control

  • Sap one target, Cheap Shot another; Kidney Shot to pin elites.
  • Blind as emergency; Gouge → Bandage to recover.
  • Kick dangerous heals/casts; dismantle (WotLK) vs weapon users.

Group Utility

  • Expose Armor if no warrior/druid; Tricks of the Trade (WotLK) on tank.
  • Coordinated interrupts and stuns keep healers safe in dungeons.
  • Distract to control patrols; Sap for clean skips.

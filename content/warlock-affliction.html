Affliction Warlock Leveling Guide

King of DoT leveling with endless sustain. Drain-tank, multi-DoT, and let your pet hold threat while you kite.

Updated 2026 DoT / Sustain DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Affliction in Classic?

Unmatched sustainability. Life Tap + Drain Life + Spirit Tap lets you chain pull forever. Voidwalker tanks early; succubus for faster kills later.

Strengths

  • Near zero downtime with Drain Life/Life Tap.
  • Multi-DoT multiple mobs and kite.
  • Strong self-healing and CC (Fear/Seduce).

Weaknesses

  • Pet micromanagement required.
  • Lower burst on short-lived mobs.
  • Needs wand early; gear for spell power.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Improved Corruption (instant) and Suppression for hit.
  • Improved Life Tap/Drain Soul for shard efficiency.
  • Nightfall for Shadow Bolt procs.

Mid (30-44)

  • Siphon Life for sustain.
  • Shadow Mastery for DoT damage.
  • Fel Concentration for pushback resistance.

Late (45-60)

  • Dark Pact for mana from pet.
  • Shadowburn (Destruction) for executes.
  • Ruin optional for crit Shadow Bolts.
Standard pull: Corruption → CoA → Siphon Life → Fear kite → Drain Life/Drain Soul; keep Life Tap rolling to convert health to mana and Drain Life back.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • Corruption (instant) → Curse (Agony) → Siphon Life.
  • Shadow Bolt on Nightfall procs; wand to finish early levels.
  • Drain Life when tapped; maintain DoTs, keep pet on target.

Multi-DoT

  • Tag 3-4 mobs with Corruption/CoA; kite with Fear.
  • Drain Life a central target; keep Voidwalker or Succubus managing threat.
  • Howl of Terror for escapes.

Survival

  • Soulstone yourself; Healthstones always crafted.
  • Use Voidwalker Sacrifice bubble when in danger.
  • Keep Demon Armor, and bandage during Fear.

Why Affliction in TBC?

Unstable Affliction, Siphon Life, and Dark Pact make Affliction a top dungeon and quest spec. Sustained damage with zero downtime.

Spec: Unstable Affliction, Siphon Life, Shadow Mastery, Contagion, Dark Pact. Destruction splash for Shadowburn/Devastation.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-63)

  • Shadow Mastery, Contagion.
  • Unstable Affliction early for dispel protection/damage.
  • Dark Pact for mana.

Mid (64-67)

  • Siphon Life; Improved Corruption/Agony.
  • Fel Concentration; Empowered Corruption.
  • Shadow Embrace for debuff.

Late (68-70)

  • Destruction splash: Bane/Devastation for Shadow Bolt.
  • Shadowburn execute.
  • Optional: Amplify Curse for burst.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • UA → Corruption → Siphon Life → CoA/Elements → Shadow Bolt filler.
  • Maintain Life Tap + Dark Pact for mana.
  • Shadowburn for execute; use Felhunter for interrupts/regen.

Multi-DoT

  • Spread UA/Corruption/CoA on 3-4 targets.
  • Fear-kite; use Howl of Terror to peel.
  • Drain Life and Dark Pact to stay topped.

Utility

  • Felhunter Silence/Devour Magic; Succubus for Seduce if PvP.
  • Soulstone/Healthstones for group.
  • Banish demons/elementals; Curse of Tongues on casters.

Why Affliction in WotLK?

Everlasting Affliction keeps Corruption rolling; Haunt adds burst and healing; Drain Soul execute is insane. One of the best levelers and dungeon specs.

Spec: Haunt, Everlasting Affliction, Eradication, Siphon Life (passive), Improved Felhunter. Destruction splash for Shadowburn.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-73)

  • Improved Corruption, Suppression, Siphon Life passive.
  • Eradication for haste procs.
  • Haunt unlock ASAP for damage/heal.

Mid (74-77)

  • Everlasting Affliction to refresh Corr with Drain/Haunt.
  • Improved Felhunter for mana return.
  • Malediction for damage; Shadow Embrace upkeep.

Late (78-80)

  • Pandemic for crit on DoTs; Death's Embrace for execute.
  • Destruction splash: Bane/Shadowburn.
  • Optional: Improved Drain Soul for mana/aggro dump.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Haunt → UA → Corruption → CoA/CoE → Siphon Life (passive) → Shadow Bolt filler.
  • Refresh Corruption via Drain Life/Soul or Haunt (Everlasting Affliction).
  • Drain Soul sub-25% for huge execute; keep Haunt up.

AoE/Multi-DoT

  • Spread Corruption/UA/CoA on 3-4 mobs; Seed of Corruption for packs.
  • Howl of Terror to peel; Shadowflame if safe.
  • Use Felhunter for mana/interrupt; Voidwalker if you need tanking.

Survival/Utility

  • Haunt healing + Siphon Life keep you topped.
  • Demon Armor for healing bonus; Glyph of Siphon Life helps.
  • Soulshatter if threat spikes; Healthstone yourself often.

Gear, Stats, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Spell Power > Hit (6-9% leveling) > Haste (WotLK) > Crit.
  • Spirit converts to spell power via Fel Armor (WotLK); helpful for regen.

Weapons

  • High-damage wand; staff/off-hand with spell power.
  • Classic: SM/BRD/Strat wands; TBC: Coilfang/Badge wands.
  • WotLK: quest/badge wands; Titansteel Guardian if needed.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Spell Power, +Spirit to weapon.
  • TBC: Major Spell Power; +Int/Spirit to gear.
  • WotLK: +63 SP/Black Magic; Icewalker if low hit.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • Multi-DoT 3-4 mobs; Drain Life to full; Life Tap often.
  • Use Felhunter for mana/interrupts; Voidwalker for heavy pulls.
  • Keep Soulstone/Healthstones ready; Soulshatter if needed.

Group Utility

  • Summoning stone, Soulstone, Healthstones.
  • Curses: Elements/Tongues situational.
  • Banish demons/elementals; Seduce for CC.

Survival

  • Demon Armor + Siphon Life healing; Drain Soul for shards while executing.
  • Fear/Howl of Terror to reset; use Demonic Circle (WotLK) to kite.
  • Carry pots/bandages; keep pet on defensive when questing.

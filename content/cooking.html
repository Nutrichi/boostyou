Tips

  • Pair with Fishing to cut costs, especially in WotLK where fish dominate recipes.
  • Cook as you level; many mobs drop meats along your class leveling routes.
  • Use cooking fires in the wild or carry flint/tinder. In cities, inns give the fire buff.
  • Sell raid foods (Dragonfin/Feasts) mid-week; prices spike before raid nights.

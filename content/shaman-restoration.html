Restoration Shaman Leveling Guide

Totem toolkit healer with strong tank saves and efficient chain healing. Best for dungeon leveling; solo is slower but extremely safe.

Updated 2026 Healer / Support Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Restoration in Classic?

Chain Heal (later) and strong single-target healing with totems make Resto reliable in dungeons. Mana can be tight but totems/Downranking help.

Strengths

  • Totems provide unique utility (Tremor, Grounding, Mana Spring).
  • Downranked Healing Wave/Chain Heal efficient.
  • Plate/ mail armor with shield for safety.

Weaknesses

  • Solo kill speed is slow.
  • Mana management required before Water Shield.
  • Totem setup time reduces speed when questing.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Improved Healing Wave/Healing Focus.
  • Tidal Focus for cheaper heals.
  • Totemic Focus for cheaper totems.

Mid (30-44)

  • Nature's Swiftness for instant heal.
  • Mana Tide not yet; Healing Way for tank healing.
  • Restorative Totems for Mana Spring strength.

Late (45-60)

  • Tidal Mastery for crit; Nature's Guidance (Enh) for hit if questing.
  • Chain Heal (if patch) for group healing.
  • Ancestral Healing for tank mitigation.
Dungeon focus: keep Earth Shield (if available later) or Riptide (WotLK) on tank; use Mana Spring/Healing Stream; downrank heals for efficiency.

Rotation & Playstyle

Solo

  • Use Flame Shock + Lightning Bolt; Searing Totem to help.
  • Heal with Lesser Healing Wave/Healing Wave as needed.
  • Water Shield (if available) or Lightning Shield for mana/damage.

Healing

  • Healing Wave for tanks; Chain Heal for groups.
  • Use NS + HW for emergencies.
  • Magma/Stoneclaw totem to control adds while healing.

Utility

  • Tremor/Poison Cleansing/Grounding situational.
  • Mana Spring/Healing Stream for sustain.
  • Purge dangerous buffs; Earth Shock interrupts.

Why Restoration in TBC?

Earth Shield, Chain Heal, and Mana Tide make Resto Shaman the best 5-man healer. Totems add huge utility and Bloodlust speeds clears.

Spec: Earth Shield, Nature's Swiftness, Mana Tide, Improved Chain Heal, Tidal Mastery. Take Totemic Focus/Restorative Totems.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-63)

  • Improved Chain Heal/Healing Way.
  • Tidal Focus/Healing Focus for efficiency/pushback.
  • Nature's Swiftness for instant heals.

Mid (64-67)

  • Earth Shield at 64-66—huge mitigation.
  • Tidal Mastery for crit; Restorative Totems for Mana Spring.
  • Mana Tide Totem for regen.

Late (68-70)

  • Nature's Guidance (Enh) for hit if questing.
  • Totem Mastery/Totemic Focus for cheaper totems.
  • Ancestral Healing for armor buff.

Rotation (TBC)

Healing

  • Keep Earth Shield on tank; Riptide not yet.
  • Chain Heal for cleave damage; LHW for spot heals.
  • Mana Tide when low; NS + HW to save tanks.

Solo

  • Flame Shock → Lightning Bolt; use Water Shield.
  • Searing Totem to supplement damage.
  • Earthbind to kite; heal yourself efficiently.

Utility

  • Tremor, Grounding, Poison Cleansing as needed.
  • Heroism/Bloodlust on bosses.
  • Purge/interrupt with Earth Shock.

Why Restoration in WotLK?

Riptide, Earth Shield, and Chain Heal remain powerful; Tidal Waves enables fast LHW/GHW casts. Strong dungeon/raid healer with huge utility.

Spec: Riptide, Tidal Waves, Improved Earth Shield, Ancestral Awakening, Mana Tide. Take Healing Way/Improved Shields.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-73)

  • Riptide unlock; Improved Earth Shield.
  • Tidal Focus/Healing Focus; Ancestral Healing.
  • Tidal Mastery for crit.

Mid (74-77)

  • Tidal Waves for fast LHW/GHW after Chain Heal/Riptide.
  • Ancestral Awakening for smart heals.
  • Meditation via Improved Water Shield for regen.

Late (78-80)

  • Mana Tide; Nature's Swiftness.
  • Optional: Cleanse Spirit for curse removal.
  • Fill with Totemic Focus/Restorative Totems.

Rotation (WotLK)

Healing

  • Keep Earth Shield on tank; Riptide on cooldown.
  • Chain Heal for group; LHW/GHW with Tidal Waves haste.
  • Mana Tide when low; NS + GHW for emergencies.

Solo/Questing

  • Flame Shock → Lightning Bolt; Fire Nova on packs.
  • Use Water Shield; drop Searing/Magma.
  • Wolves/Thunderstorm not available—kite with Earthbind.

Utility

  • Heroism/Bloodlust; Tremor/Grounding/Poison Cleansing.
  • Cleanse Spirit removes curses/poisons/diseases.
  • Earth Shock interrupts; Purge buffs.

Gear, Stats, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Intellect/MP5 > Spell Power > Crit (for Ancestral Healing) > Haste (WotLK higher value).
  • Spirit less valuable than MP5 for shamans.

Weapons

  • 1H + shield with spell power; fast weapon fine.
  • Classic: SM/BRD spell power maces; AV shield.
  • TBC: Gavel of Unearthed Secrets, Continuum Blade; Aegis of the Sunbird.
  • WotLK: Titansteel Guardian; badge/crafted shields.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Spell Power to weapon; +Int to shield.
  • TBC: Major Intellect/SP; +Spirit/MP5 to chest.
  • WotLK: +63 SP/Black Magic to weapon; +Int to shield; +MP5 to chest.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • Dungeon spam is fastest—instant queues.
  • Downrank heals and keep Water Shield up for mana.
  • Use Mana Tide and potions aggressively to avoid downtime.

Group Utility

  • Tremor/Grounding/Poison Cleansing; Mana Spring/Healing Stream.
  • Heroism/Bloodlust on bosses or big pulls.
  • Earth Shield and Ancestral Healing keep tanks sturdy.

Survival

  • NS + HW saves you or tank; Earthbind to kite.
  • Reincarnation safety net; carry Ankhs.
  • Keep bandages/pots to minimize downtime.

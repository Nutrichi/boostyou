Demonology Warlock Leveling Guide

Tanky pet-based leveling with massive survivability. Felguard (TBC) and Metamorphosis (WotLK) offer strong melee-caster hybrid play.

Updated 2026 Pet Tank / Hybrid DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Demonology in Classic?

Voidwalker tanking makes leveling safe; Demonic Embrace adds stamina; Soul Link (later) makes you very hard to kill. Slower than Affliction but great survivability.

Strengths

  • High stamina and Soul Link mitigation.
  • Voidwalker can tank elites with Drain Life support.
  • Pet utility: Succubus for Seduce, Imp for stam buff.

Weaknesses

  • Lower damage than Affliction early.
  • Shard management and pet micro.
  • Less burst; relies on pet threat.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Demonic Embrace for stamina.
  • Improved VW for threat; Fel Intellect/Stamina.
  • Improved Life Tap/Drain Life (Affliction splash).

Mid (30-44)

  • Master Demonologist for pet bonuses.
  • Fel Domination for instant pet.
  • Dark Pact optional from Affliction splash.

Late (45-60)

  • Soul Link for 30% damage split.
  • Improved Healthstone; Demonic Sacrifice optional if using succubus.
  • Ruin from Destruction for Shadow Bolt crits.
Keep VW on Defensive/Taunt; Corruption + CoA, wand/Shadow Bolt; Drain Life to keep you up. Soul Link makes you nearly unkillable.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • Send pet → Corruption/CoA → Shadow Bolt or wand.
  • Drain Life when tapped; maintain Demon Armor.
  • Use Soul Link and Healthstone liberally.

Multi-DoT

  • VW holds one target; DoT others and fear kite.
  • Use Hellfire/Rain of Fire cautiously; Health Funnel to keep pet up.
  • Howl of Terror for safety.

Survival

  • Fel Domination + Summon for instant pet swap.
  • Sacrifice from VW for shield if needed.
  • Keep bandages; use Soulstone on self.

Why Demonology in TBC?

Felguard (41-point) is incredible for leveling and dungeons. High health, cleave, and solid threat; Demonic Knowledge boosts your damage.

Spec: Felguard, Demonic Knowledge, Soul Link, Demonic Resilience. Affliction splash for UA or SL optional.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-63)

  • Demonic Embrace, Fel Stamina/Intellect.
  • Master Demonologist; Demonic Resilience.
  • Fel Domination for instant pet.

Mid (64-67)

  • Felguard unlock (~61-62).
  • Soul Link + Demonic Knowledge.
  • Improved Healthstone; Demonic Aegis.

Late (68-70)

  • Affliction splash: UA/SL for DoT power.
  • Shadowburn (Destro) for execute.
  • Improved Soul Leech not yet—use Dark Pact/Life Tap.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • Send Felguard → Corruption/UA → CoA/CoE → Shadow Bolt filler.
  • Cleave with Cleave/Intercept; keep Demonic Frenzy stacked.
  • Drain Life if low; Dark Pact for mana.

AoE

  • Seed of Corruption on multiple mobs; Felguard Cleave/Intercept.
  • Hellfire if safe (Soul Link mitigates).
  • Howl of Terror to peel; Health Funnel pet.

Utility

  • Felguard can tank adds; use Intercept to stun.
  • Felhunter swap for silence/dispel if needed.
  • Soulstone/Healthstone for group; Banish demons/elementals.

Why Demonology in WotLK?

Metamorphosis gives huge survivability and AoE; Demonic Pact buffs party. Felguard + Meta makes leveling very safe and fun.

Spec: Metamorphosis, Demonic Pact, Molten Core, Decimation, Soul Link, Demonic Empowerment. Affliction splash for Improved Corruption.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-73)

  • Improved Corruption, Suppression (Affliction).
  • Demonic Embrace, Fel Synergy (pet healing).
  • Felguard baseline; Demonic Empowerment.

Mid (74-77)

  • Molten Core (buffs Incinerate/SB).
  • Demonic Pact for spell power buff.
  • Decimation for fast Soul Fire executes.

Late (78-80)

  • Metamorphosis capstone.
  • Improved Demonic Tactics for pet crit.
  • Master Summoner for quick pet swaps.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Corruption → Immolate → Incinerate filler (with Molten Core) or Shadow Bolt if not procced.
  • Keep Curse (Doom/Agony) up; use Decimation to spam Soul Fire sub-35%.
  • Demonic Empowerment on cooldown; Metamorphosis for burst/survival.

AoE

  • Seed of Corruption; Immolation Aura in Meta for huge AoE.
  • Felguard Cleave; Shadowflame if safe.
  • Use Meta + Hellfire with Soul Link for tankiness.

Survival/Utility

  • Soul Link + Meta makes you very durable.
  • Health Funnel to keep pet alive; Fel Synergy heals pet from your damage.
  • Demonic Circle for mobility; Soulshatter to drop threat.

Gear, Stats, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Spell Power > Hit (6-9% leveling) > Haste > Crit.
  • Stamina valuable with Soul Link/Meta; spirit ok with Fel Armor.

Weapons

  • Spell power staff/off-hand; high damage wand.
  • Classic: SM/BRD/Strat; TBC: Coilfang/Badge; WotLK: Titansteel Guardian.
  • Pet scales with your stats—prioritize SP.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Spell Power weapon; +Stamina optional.
  • TBC: Major Spell Power; +Int/Spirit to gear.
  • WotLK: +63 SP/Black Magic; +Hit if needed.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • Let Felguard/VW tank; you DoT and bolt.
  • Use Meta for big pulls and safety; keep Demonic Empowerment rolling.
  • Farm shards with Drain Soul; keep Soulshatter ready.

Group Utility

  • Demonic Pact buff; Healthstones; Soulstone.
  • Banish demons/elementals; Seduce with succubus if needed.
  • Felhunter for interrupts/dispels.

Survival

  • Soul Link reduces damage; Health Funnel pet.
  • Meta gives armor and Immolation Aura; Demon Armor for healing bonus.
  • Carry pots/bandages; use Sacrifice shield if using VW.

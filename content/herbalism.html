Herbalism Leveling Guide

Fastest routes to 450 with expansion-specific herb targets, best circuits, and gold-focused tips. Pairs perfectly with Alchemy and your gathering addons.

Updated 2026 Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK Best paired with Alchemy

Classic Route (1-300)

SkillZones & HerbsNotes
1-70Starter zones: Peacebloom, Silverleaf, EarthrootLoop around your starting area; buy a 12-slot herb bag.
70-115Westfall/Darkshore/Barrens: Mageroyal, BriarthornHug rivers; Darkshore beaches are dense.
115-150Hillsbrad/Arathi: Bruiseweed, Wild SteelbloomRun the Arathi east-side cliffs for Steelbloom.
150-200Swamp of Sorrows/Desolace: Liferoot, KingsbloodStick to water edges for Liferoot; Desolace kodo areas for Kingsblood.
200-235Feralas/Hinterlands: Goldthorn, Khadgar's WhiskerHinterlands waterfall loop is efficient.
235-260Felwood: Sungrass, GromsbloodPick up Dreamfoil if it spawns; watch for Satyr aggro.
260-285Un'Goro Crater: Dreamfoil, Mountain SilversageDo outer rim clockwise; avoid devilsaurs.
285-300Eastern Plaguelands/Winterspring: Plaguebloom, IcecapPlaguebloom along EPL edges; Icecap near Winterspring cliffs.
Carry Herbalist's Gloves or glove enchant (+5) and Living Action for safety in EPL/Winterspring. Train at 50/125/200/275 in capital cities or Camp Mojache/Feathermoon.

TBC Route (300-375)

SkillZones & HerbsNotes
300-325Hellfire Peninsula: FelweedFollow cliff edges; combine with Mining if desired.
325-350Zangarmarsh: Felweed, Dreaming GloryMushroom ring circuits are hyper-dense.
350-365Terokkar Forest: Terocone, FelweedSkyrange + Firewing Point for Terocone.
365-375Nagrand/Blade's Edge: Ragveil, Dreaming Glory, NetherbloomHit Nagrand plains; swap to Netherstorm for Netherbloom at 375.
Pick up Outland Herbalism at 300. Netherbloom and Nightmare Vine can spawn elites—use mount speed bonuses and keep Swiftness Potions.

WotLK Route (375-450)

SkillZones & HerbsNotes
375-400Borean Tundra/Howling Fjord: GoldcloverStay near coastlines and tundra ponds.
400-435Sholazar Basin: Tiger Lily, Adder's TongueOuter rim lake + river loops give nonstop nodes.
435-450Storm Peaks/Icecrown: Lichbloom, IcethornDo snowy cliff circuits; bring Cold Weather Flying.
Swap to Gatherer or Routes addon to optimize node density. Adder's Tongue sells best early; Lichbloom/Icethorn stay valuable for flasks.

Setup & Gold Tips

  • Carry an herb bag + plenty of empty slots; use mailbox to offload often.
  • Alchemy synergy: craft your own potions/flasks or sell raw Adder's Tongue and Lichbloom during raid nights.
  • Use Sky Golem is not available in Classic; stick to Epic Flying + Nitro Boosts (if engineer).
  • On PvP realms, choose off-hours and low-traffic circuits (Hinterlands, Zangarmarsh mushrooms, Sholazar outer ring).

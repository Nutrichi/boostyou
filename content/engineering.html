Gold & Tips

Pairing

  • Mining to self-supply bars and stones.
  • Skinning for extra gold if dual gathering.

Profit

  • Scopes, ammo, bombs during progression.
  • Mechano-Hog/Chopper (WotLK - MoP) is big gold if you invest.
  • Explosive/Injector kits sell to raiders.

Extras

  • Use Blingtron? (later exp). For WotLK, sell Field Repair Bot 110G/74A.
  • Keep triggers/bolts banked for later crafts.
  • Quest “The Ultrasonic Screwdriver” for goggle recipes.

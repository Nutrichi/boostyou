Skinning Leveling Guide

Fastest zone hops to 600 skinning with suggested mobs and gear tips.

Updated 2026Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK / Cata / MoPPairs with Leatherworking

Classic Zones (1-300)

SkillZone/MobsNotes
1-50Elwynn/Durotar/Mulgore starter beastsUse skinning knife; don't forget to train.
50-100Westfall/The BarrensHyenas/boars.
100-150Hillsbrad/Thousand NeedlesKodo/raptors.
150-200Badlands/Swamp of SorrowsWhelps/crocs.

225-260Un'Goro CraterDinos + bonus rugged leather.
260-300Winterspring/EPLFrostsabers/Plaguehounds.
Keep skinning glove enchant + skinning knife; use level-appropriate mobs (same level) for best skill-ups.

WotLK Zones (375-450)

SkillZone/MobsNotes
375-400Borean Tundra rhinos/wolvesBorean Leather.
400-420Howling Fjord sharks/worgsStay near camp for repairs.
420-440Sholazar Basin rhinos/waspsBest Borean farm; chance at Arctic Fur.
440-450Icecrown mammoths/Storm Peaks wyrmsArctic Fur chance; max skill.

Cata Zones (450-525)

SkillZone/MobsNotes
450-480Mount Hyjal wildlife / Tol Barad PeninsulaSavage Leather starts here.
480-500Uldum — Vir'naal River crocolisksDense packs, fast respawns.
500-525Twilight Highlands wildlife + dragonkinBlackened Dragonscale from drakes.
Savage Leather (and Heavy Savage 5:1 combines) feeds all Cata Leatherworking; Pristine Hide procs are the jackpot.

MoP Zones (525-600)

SkillZone/MobsNotes
525-560Valley of the Four Winds — mushan and turtlesExotic Leather; huge mob density.
560-580Kun-Lai Summit — yaks and goatsOpen plains, easy circuits.
580-600Townlong / Dread Wastes wildlifePrismatic Scale from water/serpent mobs.
Exotic Leather is MoP's universal leather — bulk it for Magnificent Hide combines. Skinning speed pairs perfectly with the 85-90 leveling grind through the same zones.

Tips & Gold

Use Finkle's Skinner (if available) for +10 skill, or enchant gloves. Sell Arctic Fur/Heavy Borean, and convert scraps. Pair with Leatherworking for self-sufficiency.

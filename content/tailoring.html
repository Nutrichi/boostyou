Gold & Tips

Pairing

  • Enchanting for DE profits.
  • Farming humanoids for cloth.

Profit

  • Bags (Runecloth, Netherweave, Frostweave).
  • Cooldown cloth epics (WotLK) and specialty sets.
  • Spellthreads (WotLK) after rep unlocks.

Cooldowns

  • Moonshroud/Spellweave/Ebonweave (3.5 days).
  • Tailoring procs (Northrend Cloth Scavenging) boost cloth farming.

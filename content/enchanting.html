Gold & Tips

Pairing

  • Tailoring for cheap greens to disenchant.
  • Gather cloth routes (Farming humanoids).

Profit

  • Weapon/Boot enchants early in expansions sell fast.
  • Sell wands (Classic) and rods; every enchanter needs them.
  • Scrolls (WotLK - MoP) let you AH enchants—use Inks from friends or AH.

Stockpiling

  • Buy cheap greens/blues to DE during off-hours.
  • Watch for shard/dust price swaps; shatter Abyss Crystals if profitable.
  • Use vellum to bank enchants and sell later.

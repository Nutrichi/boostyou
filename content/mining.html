Mining Leveling Guide

Ore circuits, smelting breakpoints, and trainer stops to push Mining 1-450 fast. Great with Blacksmithing, Engineering, and Jewelcrafting.

Updated 2026 Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK Best paired with Blacksmithing/JC/Engineering

Classic Route (1-300)

SkillOre/ZoneNotes
1-65Copper: Elwynn/Durotar/Dun MoroghMine + smelt Copper to 65; sell bars or use for BS.
65-90Tin: Darkshore/Barrens/Loch ModanSmelt Bronze (65-90) using Copper + Tin.
90-125Iron: Wetlands/Arathi/HillsbradSmelt Iron to 125; grab some Coal for Steel later.
125-155Steel smeltingSmelt Steel (Iron + Coal) to push cheap points.
155-175Gold: Arathi/BadlandsMine/Smelt Gold; can also finish on Iron.
175-230Mithril: Tanaris/Hinterlands/Searing GorgeTanaris outer loop or Mithril stand in Searing Gorge.
230-250Truesilver pocketsSmall gains from Truesilver; keep for crafts.
250-300Thorium: Un'Goro/Winterspring/SilithusDo Un'Goro outer rim, then Winterspring for rich nodes.
Trainer at 50/125/200/275. Keep a Mining Pick, buy a cheap helm with Mithril Spurs for speed, and use Fiery Enchant on boots (Classic) for extra movement.

TBC Route (300-375)

SkillOre/ZoneNotes
300-325Fel Iron: Hellfire PeninsulaCliff edges and around Hellfire Citadel.
325-350Fel Iron/Adamantite: ZangarmarshCircle the outer swamp; lower competition.
350-365Adamantite: NagrandElemental plateau + around Halaa.
365-375Adamantite/Khorium: Nagrand/Netherstorm/SMVSwap to Netherstorm floating islands for rich nodes.
Smelt Fel Iron to 325 and Adamantite to 340+ if you want less travel. Keep Primal Earth/Fire from mote clouds if you have Engineering mote extractor.

WotLK Route (375-450)

SkillOre/ZoneNotes
375-400Cobalt: Borean Tundra/Howling FjordCoastlines and ridges; smelt to 400 if needed.
400-425Cobalt/Saronite: Dragonblight/Grizzly HillsDense Saronite in Dragonblight cliffs.
425-440Saronite: Sholazar BasinOuter rim + Maker's Perch. Extremely dense.
440-450Titanium/Saronite: Icecrown/Storm PeaksFocus on rich Saronite and Titanium; requires Cold Weather Flying.
Smelting Saronite to 440 is viable if competition is high. Save Titanium for Titansteel cooldowns (20h) and Jewelcrafting prospecting.

Prep & Gold Tips

  • Use Find Minerals tracking at all times; keep it on a macro with your mount.
  • Smelt for cheap skill-ups when ore is expensive. Check if bars sell higher than raw ore on your realm.
  • Prospecting (Jewelcrafting) multiplies profits on Adamantite/Saronite. Consider mailing ore to your JC alt.
  • Farm during off-hours to avoid node sniping. Pair with RestedXP routes to bank XP while gathering.

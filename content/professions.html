Professions overview

See which professions are available per expansion and jump to the right guide — every guide now covers skill 1-600 through Cata and MoP. Jewelcrafting unlocks in TBC, Inscription in WotLK.

Every profession below has a full guide — click through for expansion-specific leveling paths and gold tips.

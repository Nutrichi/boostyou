TBC Phase 1 BiS lists are live for all 27 specs — Karazhan, Gruul & Magtheridon

The BiS browser now covers TBC Anniversary Phase 1 for every class and spec: full Tier 4 best-in-slot lists built around Karazhan, Gruul's Lair and Magtheridon's Lair, with badge rewards, crafted pieces, reputation items and world boss drops filling the gaps.

Phase 1 is where TBC's crafted gear shines, and the lists show it: Shadow Priests and Frost Mages live in Tailoring (Spellstrike and Frozen Shadoweave), Warlocks pair four-piece Voidheart with Spellstrike Pants, and Elemental Shamans mix Tier 4 with Leatherworking's Netherstrike set. Hunters keep the five-piece Beast Lord dungeon set all phase for its set bonus.

Where a slot has real alternatives — mages and druids especially — the lists rank up to three options, so you know what to grab while you wait for that one Prince Malchezaar drop. Tier token bosses are marked on every tier piece.

A few lists mention Mark of the Champion from Classic-era Naxxramas: that is a legacy item for characters who carried it over, not something you can farm — it is flagged as such.

The lists are marked as drafts while we verify every source — spot something off? Tell us and we'll fix it fast.

Browse your spec at https://boostyou.ai/content/bis.html — pick TBC, Phase 1, your class and spec. Every item links to Wowhead.

With Pre-Raid, Phase 1 and Phase 2 all covered, TBC Anniversary now has a complete gearing path from fresh 70 to Lady Vashj and Kael'thas — ready for whenever you roll that alt.

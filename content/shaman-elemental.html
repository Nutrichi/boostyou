Elemental Shaman Leveling Guide

Ranged burst caster with strong control and totem utility. Lightning Bolt/Chain Lightning provide excellent kill speed with manageable downtime.

Updated 2026 Ranged Burst DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Elemental in Classic?

High single-target burst with Elemental Mastery and Fire Nova totem AoE. Mana can be tight early, but Water Shield + clearcasting keeps you going.

Strengths

  • Big crits with Elemental Mastery.
  • Totems provide utility: Grounding, Tremor, Stoneskin.
  • Decent kiting with Frost Shock and Earthbind.

Weaknesses

  • Mana hungry until Water Shield.
  • Totem setup time on short pulls.
  • Gear reliant on spell power.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Convection/Concussion for mana/damage.
  • Improved Fire Totems/Call of Flame if using Fire Nova.
  • Elemental Focus (clearcasting) at 20.

Mid (30-44)

  • Reverberation for shorter shocks.
  • Elemental Fury for crit multiplier.
  • Call of Thunder for crit; Eye of the Storm for pushback.

Late (45-60)

  • Elemental Mastery at 40 for guaranteed crit.
  • Lightning Mastery for cast speed.
  • Restoration splash: Totemic Focus/Improved Reincarnation, Nature's Swiftness.
Open with LB → CL (if 2 targets) → Shock; use EM for instant crits on tough mobs; drop Searing/Fire Nova on stacked mobs.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • Lightning Bolt spam; Earth Shock to finish.
  • Frost Shock to kite; EM + Chain Lightning for burst.
  • Keep Water Shield active to sustain mana.

AoE

  • Fire Nova + Magma totem on stacked packs.
  • Chain Lightning cleave; Stoneclaw to peel.
  • Earthbind to kite melee.

Survival

  • Use Grounding vs casters; Tremor vs fears.
  • Reincarnation as safety; Lesser Healing Wave for spot heals.
  • Carry mana pots; downrank Lightning Bolt if needed.

Why Elemental in TBC?

Totem of Wrath, clearcasting, and strong scaling make Elemental great for 5-mans and questing. Mana issues ease with Water Shield + clearcasting.

Spec: Totem of Wrath, Lightning Mastery, Elemental Mastery, Unrelenting Storm. Restoration splash for Nature's Swiftness.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-63)

  • Elemental Focus, Concussion, Convection.
  • Lightning Mastery for cast speed.
  • Unrelenting Storm for regen.

Mid (64-67)

  • Elemental Mastery; Totem of Wrath.
  • Reverberation for shock CD.
  • Call of Thunder/Elemental Fury for crit.

Late (68-70)

  • Restoration: Nature's Swiftness, Totemic Focus.
  • Improved Shields for Water Shield value.
  • Ancestral Knowledge for mana pool if needed.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • LB spam; CL on cooldown.
  • Keep Water Shield; EM for burst.
  • Earth Shock rank appropriate to finish.

AoE

  • CL + Fire Nova/Magma on packs.
  • Stoneclaw totem to peel; Grounding vs casters.
  • Use NS + CL for instant burst on dangerous pulls.

Utility

  • Tremor/Poison Cleansing/Healing Stream situational.
  • Totem of Wrath for party hit/crit.
  • Purge/Interrupt (Earth Shock) key casts.

Why Elemental in WotLK?

Lava Burst guarantees crits with Flame Shock, giving consistent burst. Thunderstorm adds mana + knockback. Excellent dungeon and quest pace.

Spec: Lava Burst, Elemental Oath, Lightning Mastery, Convection, Storm, Earth and Fire. Thunderstorm for mana/knockback; Restoration splash for NS.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-73)

  • Concussion/Convection, Elemental Focus.
  • Lava Burst + Flame Shock combo.
  • Storm, Earth and Fire for FS duration/snare.

Mid (74-77)

  • Elemental Oath for party crit buff.
  • Lightning Mastery/Overload for procs.
  • Unrelenting Storm for regen.

Late (78-80)

  • Thunderstorm for mana and control.
  • Restoration splash: NS, Improved Shields.
  • Shamanistic Rage not available—use Thunderstorm for mana.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Flame Shock → Lava Burst (guaranteed crit) → Lightning Bolt filler.
  • Chain Lightning when 2+ mobs; refresh Flame Shock.
  • Use Thunderstorm for mana and spacing.

AoE

  • CL + Magma/Fire Nova; Thunderstorm to reset packs.
  • Fire Elemental totem on big pulls; Earthbind to kite.
  • Keep Lightning Shield/Water Shield depending on mana needs.

Survival

  • NS + Healing Wave for emergency heal.
  • Grounding/Tremor/Poison Cleansing totems for mechanics.
  • Thunderstorm knockback to disengage.

Gear, Stats, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Spell Power > Hit (6-9% leveling) > Crit > Intellect/MP5 > Haste (more value in WotLK).
  • Elemental Oath rewards crit stacking; Intellect fuels mana.

Weapons

  • 1H + shield/off-hand with spell power; staff is fine early.
  • Classic: SM/BRD caster weapons; AV shield.
  • TBC: Continuum Blade, Shockwave trinkets; crafted spell power weapons.
  • WotLK: Titansteel Guardian, quest/badge maces and shields.

Enchants

  • Classic: +Spell Power weapon; +Int to bracers.
  • TBC: Major Spell Power; +Int/+MP5 to shield.
  • WotLK: +63 SP/Black Magic; +Int to shield; Icewalker if hit is low.

Pro Tips & Utility

Leveling Speed

  • Keep Water/Lightning Shield up; use Thunderstorm on cooldown for mana (WotLK).
  • Use EM/NS for instant kills on dangerous mobs.
  • Chain pull with Searing totem ticking between mobs.

Group Utility

  • Tremor/Grounding/Poison Cleansing; Totem of Wrath (TBC).
  • Heroism/Bloodlust (if grouped) for boss bursts.
  • Reincarnation for wipes; Ankh always stocked.

Survival

  • Earthbind kiting; Stoneclaw to peel.
  • NS heal + pot saves lives; use Frost Shock to slow.
  • Carry mana pots/bandages to reduce downtime.

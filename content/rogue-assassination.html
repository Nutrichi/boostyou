Assassination Rogue Leveling Guide

Poison-driven burst, strong single-target damage, and efficient leveling with control. Covers Classic, TBC, and WotLK Assassination paths for 1-80.

Updated 2026 Melee / Poison DPS Supports Classic / TBC / WotLK

Why Assassination in Classic?

Strong poison synergy, dependable crits, and solid single-target kill speed. Less energy intensive than Combat’s Sinister Strike spam; excellent for elite killing with control.

Strengths

  • Poison damage scales well; efficient energy usage.
  • Deadly/Crippling poisons give control and sustained DPS.
  • Great opener burst with Cheap Shot → Mutilate (later).

Weaknesses

  • Weapon upgrades crucial (daggers); positional (back) for some skills.
  • AoE weaker than Combat blade flurry/cleave.
  • Energy pooling required for finishers.

Talents by Level (Classic 1-60)

Early (10-29)

  • Improved Eviscerate 3/3, Malice 5/5.
  • Ruthlessness 3/3, Murder 2/2.
  • Relentless Strikes (energy refund) and Lethality.

Mid (30-44)

  • Vigor optional; Improve Poisons 5/5.
  • Cold Blood for burst; Seal Fate for combo point generation.
  • Opportunity 2/5 if needed earlier; Fill Precision in Combat if hit is low.

Late (45-60)

  • Combat splash: Dual Wield Spec, Precision 5/5, Deflection.
  • Blade Flurry optional if heavy cleave desired (requires points).
  • Finish with Improved Kidney Shot/Setup if PvP/CC focus.
Daggers strongly preferred; keep Deadly Poison on main-hand, Crippling on off-hand for control; swap to Instant/Deadly when safe.

Rotation & Playstyle

Single Target

  • Stealth → Cheap Shot or Garrote → Mutilate (or Backstab/Sinister early).
  • Slice and Dice first (2-3 CP) → Mutilate → Envenom/Eviscerate.
  • Maintain poisons; Kick important casts; Gouge → Bandage if needed.

Survival

  • Evasion vs melee elites; Vanish to reset; Blind for control.
  • Crippling Poison for kiting; Sprint for disengage.
  • First Aid maxed; use food buffs.

AoE/Cleave

  • Fan of Knives not in Classic; AoE limited—use Blade Flurry if taken.
  • Focus on single-target burn; Sap one mob when possible.

Why Assassination in TBC?

Improved poisons and Mutilate provide strong single-target. Still behind Combat for raids, but great for leveling/quests with reliable damage.

Spec: Deep Assassination to Mutilate/Deadly Brew equivalents; Combat off-tree for Precision/Dual Wield Spec; optionally Fleet Footed for mobility.

Talents by Level (TBC 60-70)

Early (60-64)

  • Improved Poisons 5/5; Vile Poisons.
  • Malice 5/5; Ruthlessness/Relentless Strikes.
  • Mutilate at 50pt; Cold Blood for burst.

Mid (65-67)

  • Seal Fate 5/5 for extra CP on crits.
  • Opportunity 2/5 if needed; Fleet Footed optional.
  • Fill Combat: Precision 5/5, Dual Wield Spec.

Late (68-70)

  • Quick Recovery for Envenom efficiency.
  • Filler: Deadened Nerves, Vitality in Combat tree.

Rotation (TBC)

Single Target

  • Garrote → Mutilate → Slice and Dice (2-3 CP).
  • Maintain SnD; Envenom as main finisher (with Deadly Poison stacks).
  • Cold Blood + Mutilate/Envenom for elites; Kick casts.

AoE

  • Fan of Knives not available; rely on single-target and Blade Flurry if taken.
  • Use Gouge/Sap for CC; Evasion tank briefly if needed.

Survival

  • Vanish to reset; Cloak of Shadows to remove magic.
  • Crippling on off-hand for control; Deadly/Instant on main-hand.
  • Bandage; use Health Potions on tough pulls.

Why Assassination in WotLK?

Mutilate + Hunger for Blood/Envenom spam with high poison uptime makes Assassination a top-tier single-target spec. Great leveling once you reach crit/haste thresholds.

Spec: Deep Assassination for Mutilate, Hunger for Blood (pre-3.3) or Cut to the Chase, Improved Poisons, Focused Attacks. Off-tree Combat for Precision/Dual Wield Spec.

Talents by Level (70-80)

Early (70-74)

  • Improved Poisons/Vile Poisons; Malice; Ruthlessness.
  • Mutilate core; Focused Attacks for energy on crit.
  • Deadly Brew optional for PvP/world control.

Mid (75-77)

  • Cut to the Chase (refresh SnD on Envenom).
  • Hunger for Blood (if applicable) or fill CttC/HfB replacements.
  • Seal Fate for extra CP.

Late (78-80)

  • Combat off-tree: Precision, Dual Wield Spec, Close Quarters Combat.
  • Optional: Fleet Footed for mobility; Deadened Nerves for mitigation.

Rotation (WotLK)

Single Target

  • Stealth → Garrote → Mutilate → Slice and Dice (2-3 CP).
  • Maintain Deadly/Instant poisons; Envenom as main finisher; Cut to the Chase keeps SnD up.
  • Use Cold Blood with Envenom/Mutilate on elites; Vanish for Overkill buff.

AoE

  • Fan of Knives with double Instant/Deadly; keep Tricks of the Trade on tank.
  • Use Blade Flurry only if Combat off-tree (unlikely here).

Survival

  • Cloak of Shadows, Evasion, Feint to reduce AoE damage.
  • Crippling Poison for kiting; Sprint/Prep for mobility.
  • Always keep a stack of bandages/health potions.

Gear, Weapons, and Enchants (All Versions)

Stat Priority

  • Hit (6-9% leveling) > Agility > AP > Crit > Haste; Expertise helps smooth uptime.
  • Daggers: fast off-hand for poison procs, slow main-hand for Mutilate.

Weapons

  • Classic: VanCleef/SM/BRD daggers; AV rewards.
  • TBC: Felsteel Longblade, Latro’s if off-hand, quest blues.
  • WotLK: Heroic daggers, crafted (Librarian’s Paper Cutter off-hand), Titansteel Shanker.

Enchants

  • Classic: Crusader/Agility; +Hit where available.
  • TBC: Mongoose/+Agility; +Hit to gloves if needed.
  • WotLK: Berserking/+Agility to weapons; Icewalker if low hit.

Pro Tips & Control

Leveling Speed

  • Open with Garrote for bleed uptime; keep SnD rolling.
  • Use Cheap Shot → Kidney on dangerous mobs.
  • Gather mobs near your level; avoid heavy armor targets when possible.

Control

  • Sap one, Cheap Shot another; Blind as emergency stop.
  • Vanish to reset; Prep (if available) for double CDs.
  • Kick to shut down healers/casters; Gouge → Bandage.

Group Utility

  • Expose Armor if no warrior/druid; Tricks of the Trade (WotLK) to tank.
  • Wound/Anesthetic Poison situational; Crippling for kiting.
  • Provide interrupts/stuns for dangerous casts.

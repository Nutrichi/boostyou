Survival is the weakest hunter leveling spec. It offers no significant advantages over Beast Mastery or Marksmanship, and both of those specs level faster. Survival becomes viable in TBC/WotLK but remains suboptimal for leveling. Choose Beast Mastery for best results.

Why Survival Falls Short

  • No damage boost: Talents focus on utility, not raw DPS
  • Trap reliance: Setting traps mid-combat slows down killing speed
  • Melee focus: You lose DPS going into melee range as a hunter
  • Better alternatives exist: BM is faster, MM has higher burst
  • Shines in PvP, not PvE: Survival talents are PvP-focused

When Survival Makes Sense

Only consider Survival if:

  • You want challenge/variety: Different playstyle for alt leveling
  • Heavy AoE grinding: Explosive Trap talents help with packs (WotLK)
  • WotLK 70-80: Survival becomes much stronger with Explosive Shot
  • Fun over efficiency: You enjoy trap gameplay and don't care about speed

üî• WotLK Exception

Survival becomes top-tier DPS in WotLK! Explosive Shot makes Survival the highest DPS hunter spec at max level. For 70-80 leveling in WotLK, Survival is actually competitive with BM. However, for Classic and TBC, stick with Beast Mastery.

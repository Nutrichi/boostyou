‚ö° Why Beast Mastery is Best for Leveling

Beast Mastery is the undisputed king of hunter leveling. Your pet becomes incredibly powerful with talents, rarely dies, and holds threat effortlessly. You have virtually no downtime, can solo elite quests, and level faster than any other hunter spec. This is the recommended spec for all leveling scenarios including Hardcore.

Talent Build Priority

Point-by-point breakdown of optimal talent allocation

üìä Classic (1-60) Talent Priority

Levels 10-20: Beast Mastery Foundation

  1. Improved Aspect of the Hawk (5/5) - Level 10-14: +30% ranged attack speed, massive DPS increase
  2. Endurance Training (5/5) - Level 15-19: +25 pet HP per point, keeps pet alive
  3. Unleashed Fury (5/5) - Level 20-24: +20% pet damage, huge power spike

Levels 25-40: Pet Specialization

  1. Bestial Swiftness (1/1) - Level 25: +30% pet movement speed, better mobility
  2. Ferocity (5/5) - Level 26-30: +10% crit chance for pet, consistent damage
  3. Bestial Discipline (2/2) - Level 31-32: +20% focus regen, more special attacks
  4. Intimidation (1/1) - Level 33: Stun ability, emergency tool
  5. Spirit Bond (2/2) - Level 34-35: Heal you and pet, reduces downtime
  6. Frenzy (5/5) - Level 36-40: +30% attack speed proc, massive damage

Levels 41-50: Maximize Pet Power

  1. Bestial Wrath (1/1) - Level 41: HUGE 31-point talent, +50% pet damage 18 sec
  2. Improved Revive Pet (2/2) - Level 42-43: Faster pet rez, quality of life
  3. Thick Hide (3/3) - Level 44-46: +20% pet armor, tankier pet
  4. Move to Marksmanship tree
  5. Lethal Shots (5/5) - Level 47-51: +5% crit, personal DPS increase

Levels 51-60: Final Optimization

  1. Aimed Shot (1/1) - Level 52: Strong opener ability
  2. Improved Hunter's Mark (5/5) - Level 53-57: +20% AP from Mark
  3. Efficiency (5/5) - Level 58-62 (if leveling to 70): -10% mana cost

Best Pets for Beast Mastery

üê± Cat (DPS - Recommended)

Why it's best: Highest DPS pet family in the game. Fast attack speed, strong abilities, perfect for fast leveling.

Where to Tame:

  • Level 10: Any cat in starting zones (Mulgore, Elwynn Forest)
  • Level 20: Stranglethorn cats (great variety)
  • Level 37: The Rake in Mulgore (rare spawn, very strong)
  • Level 60: Winterspring cats (best stats)

Abilities:

  • Claw (main damage ability)
  • Prowl (stealth - mostly cosmetic)
  • Dash (movement speed burst)

üê∫ Wolf (Utility)

Why it's good: Furious Howl buff (+40 AP to party), solid DPS, great for dungeon groups.

Where to Tame:

  • Level 10: Timber wolves in Silverpine Forest
  • Level 47: Snarler in Feralas (rare spawn, Furious Howl)
  • Level 60: ZG wolves (best stats + Furious Howl)

Abilities:

  • Bite (solid damage)
  • Furious Howl (party buff - level 47+)

üê¢ Turtle (Hardcore)

Why for HC: Highest armor, natural damage reduction, Shell Shield ability. Almost unkillable.

Where to Tame:

  • Level 15: Turtles in Wetlands (Alliance)
  • Level 20: Turtles in Barrens (Horde)
  • Level 35: Old Ironjaw in Wetlands (rare, very tanky)

Abilities:

  • Shell Shield (+50% armor for 12 sec)
  • Bite (damage)

üêó Boar (Early Leveling)

Why early: High HP pool, easy to find, very forgiving for new hunters. Great levels 10-20.

Where to Tame:

  • Level 10: Bellygrub in Durotar (Horde - rare spawn)
  • Level 10: Large Crag Boar in Westfall (Alliance)

Abilities:

  • Charge (gap closer)
  • Gore (damage)

Combat Rotation & Strategy

‚öîÔ∏è Standard Leveling Rotation

1. Send Pet ‚Üí Hunter's Mark

Send pet first to establish threat, immediately apply Hunter's Mark for bonus AP. Let pet get 1-2 hits before you shoot.

2. Serpent Sting

Apply your DoT immediately. This is your most mana-efficient damage - always use it first.

3. Auto Shot + Arcane Shot

Spam Arcane Shot on cooldown between Auto Shots. This is your main damage filler while leveling.

4. Multi-Shot (2+ enemies)

If fighting 2 or more enemies, use Multi-Shot on cooldown. Very efficient for AoE situations.

5. Bestial Wrath (Hard targets)

Use your 31-point talent on elites, rares, or packs of enemies. +50% pet damage for 18 seconds is massive.

6. Intimidation (Emergency)

If pet is losing threat or you're in danger, use Intimidation stun to reset the situation.

üéØ Advanced Tips

  • Aspect of the Hawk: Always active while leveling for DPS
  • Feed pet between pulls: Keep happiness at green/yellow minimum, green is best
  • Mend Pet: Use during combat if pet drops below 50% HP
  • Feign Death: Emergency escape if you pull too many mobs
  • Don't melee: Stay at range always - you do way more damage with ranged
  • Ammo matters: Buy the best ammo you can afford - it's a huge DPS increase

Stat Priority & Gear

üìä Stat Priority (1-60)

  1. Agility: +1 Ranged AP, increases crit chance - your main stat
  2. Stamina: Survivability, especially important for Hardcore
  3. Attack Power: Direct damage increase for you and pet
  4. Crit: More crits = faster kills
  5. Hit: Less important while leveling (mobs are at/below your level)
  6. Intellect: Bigger mana pool, less drinking

‚öôÔ∏è Gear Tips

  • Weapon: Upgrade your ranged weapon ASAP - biggest DPS increase
  • Ammo: Use best ammo available - don't cheap out
  • Mail at 40: Equip mail armor for huge survivability boost
  • Quest rewards: Prioritize Agility > Stamina > AP items
  • Dungeons: Not required, but some great items (Blackcrow from SM)
  • Enchants: Not necessary while leveling, save gold

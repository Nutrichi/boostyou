The ultimate solo leveling class. Master Beast Mastery, Marksmanship, and Survival for Classic, TBC, and WotLK

üèÜ Why Hunter is the Best Leveling Class

Hunters are widely considered the fastest and easiest class to level in WoW Classic, TBC, and WotLK. Your pet tanks while you deal damage from range, you have zero downtime, excellent survivability with Feign Death, and can solo content other classes can't touch. Whether you're going for speed or safety, Hunter dominates leveling.

Hunter Leveling Tips (All Specs)

üêæ Pet Management

  • Tame early: Get your pet at level 10 immediately
  • Keep fed: Always carry pet food matching your pet's diet
  • Loyalty matters: Keep happiness at max for full damage
  • Learn abilities: Tame beasts to learn new pet skills
  • Best pets: Cat (DPS), Wolf (utility), Bear (tanking)

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Rotation

  • Send pet first: Let pet establish threat
  • Hunter's Mark: Always use on targets
  • Serpent Sting: Apply DoT first
  • Steady Shot/Auto: Main damage filler
  • Arcane Shot: Use when available
  • Multi-Shot: Use vs 2+ enemies

üéØ Stat Priority

  • 1. Agility: Main stat - increases AP and crit
  • 2. Stamina: Survivability, especially HC
  • 3. Attack Power: Direct damage increase
  • 4. Crit: More crits = faster kills
  • 5. Hit: Don't miss shots (less important while leveling)

‚öôÔ∏è Gear Tips

  • Weapon speed matters: Slower = harder hits
  • Upgrade bow/gun/crossbow: Huge DPS increase
  • Mail at 40: Huge armor boost
  • Quest rewards: Prioritize agility/stamina gear
  • Don't skip ammo: Buy best ammo you can afford

Best Hunter Pets by Level

üê∫ Recommended Pet Progression

Level 10-20: Boar (Bellygrub in Durotar, Large Crag Boar in Westfall) - High HP, easy to manage

Level 20-30: Cat (Any cat in Stranglethorn or Thousand Needles) - Best DPS for leveling

Level 30-40: Ravager (The Rake in Mulgore if Alliance) - Iconic rare spawn, excellent DPS

Level 40-60: Wolf (Snarler in Feralas, any ZG wolf) - Furious Howl buff for groups, solid DPS

Hardcore: Turtle or Bear - Maximum survivability, almost unkillable pet

TBC/WotLK: Cat or Ravager remain best for leveling DPS

TBC Anniversary Phase 2 is live: Serpentshrine Cavern & Tempest Keep

Overlords of Outland has arrived on the Anniversary realms. Serpentshrine Cavern and Tempest Keep: The Eye are open, which means Lady Vashj and Kael'thas Sunstrider are finally in reach — and with them Tier 5 and the first real gear check of TBC.

Arena Season 2 kicks off alongside the raids, and the Ogri'la and Sha'tari Skyguard daily hubs are unlocked for everyone who wants to grind reputation (and gold) between raid nights.

If you still have alts stuck in Azeroth, now is the moment to push them to 70: raid groups are forming everywhere and heroic dungeon runs are filling up fast. Check our TBC 60-70 speed route to catch up before the Tier 5 progression race settles.

WoW Classic Era BiS lists are live — all 27 specs, every phase, Pre-Raid through Naxxramas

The BiS browser now covers the original WoW Classic (2019) release in full: Pre-Raid, Phase 1 (Molten Core & Onyxia's Lair), Phase 2, Phase 3 (Blackwing Lair), Phase 4, Phase 5 (Ahn'Qiraj) and Phase 6 (Naxxramas) — 189 lists across every class and spec.

Two honest notes on sourcing: our Phase 2 slot is filled with Icy Veins' own "refined" Molten Core/Onyxia list (with PvP and world boss items added) rather than a dedicated Dire Maul list — no such table exists on the source. Our Phase 4 slot uses Icy Veins' "Phase 4" table, which the guide itself describes as covering "Zul'Gurub and Blackwing Lair" gear together. Both are called out directly in the affected lists' notes.

One more thing worth knowing if you're new to Classic: there are no gem sockets in this era at all — Blizzard didn't add those until Cataclysm, so don't go looking for a gems section here.

Browse your spec at https://boostyou.ai/content/bis.html — pick Classic, your phase, your class and spec. Every item links to Wowhead.

The lists are marked as drafts while we verify every source — spot something off? Tell us and we'll fix it fast.

With this, Classic Era joins TBC as a fully-covered expansion on the site.

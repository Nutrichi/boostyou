MoP Classic opens Siege of Orgrimmar

The final tier of Mists of Pandaria Classic is here. Siege of Orgrimmar is open on all MoP Classic realms, sending us after Garrosh Hellscream through fourteen bosses — the longest single raid of the entire Classic journey so far.

Flex, Normal and Heroic difficulties are all available, and Timeless Isle catch-up gear makes it surprisingly quick to get an alt raid-ready.

We are updating our MoP 85-90 leveling route with the latest XP tuning, and BiS lists for the Siege of Orgrimmar phase are in the works. If you have been waiting for the right time to come back to Pandaria, this is it.

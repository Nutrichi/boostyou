MoP Tier 14 BiS lists are live for all 34 specs — Vaults, Heart of Fear & Terrace

The BiS browser now covers MoP Classic Phase 1 for every class and spec: full Tier 14 Heroic best-in-slot lists spanning Mogu'shan Vaults, Heart of Fear and Terrace of Endless Spring — including Monks and all four Druid specs.

Every tier piece is marked with its token boss, and the pattern holds across all classes: helms from Sha of Fear, shoulders from Lei Shi, chests from Grand Empress Shek'zeer, hands from Wind Lord Mel'jarak and legs from Amber-Shaper Un'sok. Some specs run five tier pieces, others swap the chest or legs for a stronger off-piece — the lists show exactly which.

Weapon setups get the full treatment too: Titan's Grip Fury with two copies of Shin'ka, dual-wield builds for Frost Death Knights, Enhancement, Combat Rogues, Windwalkers and Brewmasters (with the two-handed Gao-Rei alternative noted), and the Darkmoon Faire trinkets (Relic of Xuen, Yu'lon, Niuzao) where they beat raid drops.

The lists are marked as drafts while we verify every source — spot something off? Tell us and we'll fix it fast.

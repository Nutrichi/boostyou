The BiS browser is complete — all 757 lists, every expansion, every phase, every spec

Today the last gap closed: WotLK Phase 1 for Paladin, Hunter, Rogue and Death Knight. Icy Veins never published Phase 1 tables for those four classes, so these 12 lists come from Wowhead's WotLK Classic Phase 1 BiS guides instead — the source is named in each list's notes, as always.

That completes the full grid: Classic Era (189), The Burning Crusade (162), Wrath of the Lich King (150), Cataclysm (120) and Mists of Pandaria (136) — 757 Best in Slot lists covering Pre-Raid through the final raid tier of each expansion, for every class and spec, with drop sources, enchants and gems on every list.

Everything is drafted from established guide sources and marked with a draft badge until it has been hand-verified; verification is ongoing and badges will disappear list by list. If you spot something that looks off, each list's notes name the exact source it was built from.

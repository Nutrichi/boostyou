TBC Phase 5 BiS lists are live for all 27 specs — Sunwell Plateau

The BiS browser now covers TBC Classic Phase 5: full Sunwell Plateau best-in-slot lists for every class and spec, drafted from the original 2007-2008 release — the same approach we used for Phase 3 and 4, well ahead of the TBC Anniversary realm actually reaching this content.

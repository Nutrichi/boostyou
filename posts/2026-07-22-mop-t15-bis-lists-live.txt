MoP Tier 15 BiS lists are live for all 34 specs — Throne of Thunder is fully covered

The BiS browser now covers MoP Classic Phase 2: full Throne of Thunder Heroic best-in-slot lists for every class and spec, including Monks and all four Druid specs.

Throne of Thunder's tier sets follow the usual pattern — Dark Animus, Iron Qon, Council of Elders, Ji-Kun and Twin Consorts all drop pieces of your class's Tier 15 set, and each list marks exactly which slot comes from which boss. Weapon setups get full coverage too: dual Wu-Lai fans for Windwalker and Brewmaster Monks and Enhancement Shaman, dual daggers for Assassination and Subtlety Rogues, and the Frost Death Knight, Combat Rogue and Fury Warrior dual-wield and two-hand picks.

A few sourcing quirks worth knowing: Fire Mage uses the fully-progressed itemization (its Jadefire Drape cloak is a legendary questline reward, not a boss drop), and a couple of Icy Veins mislabels were corrected against the actual encounter list — Jin'rokh the Breaker is Throne of Thunder's own first boss (not Heart of Fear), and Lei Shen's Orb of Command drops from Lei Shen (not Feng the Accursed).

The lists are marked as drafts while we verify every source — spot something off? Tell us and we'll fix it fast.

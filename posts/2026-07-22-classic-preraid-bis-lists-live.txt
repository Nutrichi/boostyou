Classic Era Pre-Raid BiS lists are live — Holy/Discipline Priest and Protection Warrior

The BiS browser now includes a first taste of standard WoW Classic (vanilla): Phase 1 pre-raid best-in-slot gear for Holy/Discipline Priest healers and Protection Warrior tanks, sourced from Warcraft Tavern's community guides.

Every item here comes from level 51-60 five-man dungeons — Blackrock Depths, Upper and Lower Blackrock Spire, Scholomance, Stratholme, Sunken Temple, Maraudon, Uldaman — plus world drops and quest chains. No raid content required, just solid dungeon farming before Molten Core opens up.

Browse at https://boostyou.ai/content/bis.html — pick Classic, Pre-Raid, your class and spec. Every item links to Wowhead.

Heads up: this is a small first batch. Classic Era doesn't have the same structured, per-spec gear guides that our other expansions do, so filling out the remaining 24 pre-raid specs — and all six raid phases from Molten Core through Naxxramas — will take some extra source-hunting. More coming as we track those down.

The lists are marked as drafts while we verify every source — spot something off? Tell us and we'll fix it fast.
